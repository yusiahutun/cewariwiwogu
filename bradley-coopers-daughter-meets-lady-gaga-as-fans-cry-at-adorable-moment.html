Bradley Cooper's daughter meets Lady Gaga as fans 'cry' at adorable moment

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Bradley Cooper’s daughter, Lea De Seine has made her Red Carpet debut at the Maestro premiere. The 48-year-old walked the carpet with his six-year-old, who he shares with ex, Irina Shayk.

The Hollywood star attended the premiere of his latest movie, Maestro. The flick will be streaming on Netflix from December 20. Bradley Cooper was also reunited with A Star is Born co-star Lady Gaga, who could be seen mingling with the father-daughter duo on the night.

Bradley Cooper’s daughter makes red carpet debut

With two celebrity parents, it’s no wonder the six-year-old is a natural on the Red Carpet.

Lea posed like a natural at the premiere, holding hands with her father. She wore an adorable animal print dress, paired with sparkling gold ballet pumps.

She added a pop of color with a bright red bag, and the lipstick to match. Bradley looked dapper alongside his daughter, wearing a dark blue suit.

Lea is The Hangover star’s only child, born in 2017. He and Irina called it quits two years later, however, they remain on good terms.

Taking to her Instagram story, the model and TV personality wrote: “Congrats @maestrofilm. So proud daddy and Lea.”

Cooper directed and co-wrote the film, as well as starring in it, alongside Carey Mulligan. He plays the late West Side Story composer, Leonard Bernstein.

Although Brad chose his daughter as his plus-one, he’s currently rumored to be dating model Gigi Hadid. The rumored celebrity couple have been spotted out and about a few times, most recently at his New York food truck.

However, they’re yet to make their Red Carpet debut, but we know all the fans will be waiting.

Lady Gaga shows support for her ‘friend for life’

Five years after their hit movie A Star is Born was released, Bradley Cooper and Lady Gaga were reunited, as she could be seen mingling with his daughter, Lea.

Fans on X, formerly known as Twitter, are going wild for the adorable pic.

“YOU DON’T UNDERSTAND!! I AM THE HAPPIEST RIGHT NOW!! [crying emojis]” wrote one.

Another simply exclaimed: OHHHH MY GODDD.”

The pair became extremely close during the filming of the blockbuster, as the 48-year-old said he had “made a friend for life.”

“I made a friend for life. As great as this movie was the thing that I think I’ll take away forever is the relationship I have with her because she is an incredible person. I’m very lucky to have worked with her,” he said in an interview with PEOPLE.

The music icon stunned in a chic black blazer and pants set, with some funky silver embellishments, which appeared to resemble angels.
