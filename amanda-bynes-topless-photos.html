Amanda Bynes Posts Topless Photos, Says She's Not Crazy

June 2024 · 1 minute read

Amanda Bynes has upped her Twitter game -- going from bra photos to posing topless in a matter of 24 hours.

The actress has been chronicling some bizarre behavior on the social networking site, sharing pics of her buzzing her hair, hitting the gym and posting videos of her making weird faces for the camera.

But last night, she shared her raciest shot yet, one of her holding her breasts while wearing nothing but underwear and tights. "Rawr!" she posted along with the pic.

The photos come on the heels of a new interview with In Touch, where she says she's not a crazy person.

"I have no clue [why people say I'm insane]. Every time I've heard it, it came from an ugly person's mouth, so I don't care,” she told the mag. "The only ones lying about me having a mental illness are people I don't talk to."
