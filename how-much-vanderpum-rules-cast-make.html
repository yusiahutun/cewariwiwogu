How Much Does Vanderpump Rules Cast Make? Salary Per Episode 2024

April 2024 · 18 minute read

As one of the most popular shows on Bravo, it’s understandable why there’s been so much interest in how much the Vanderpump Rules‘ cast makes and the salaries per episode for stars like Ariana Madix, Tom Sandoval, and Lisa Vanderpump. You may be surprised at which Vanderpump Rules cast member makes the most (and who makes the least). Keep reading for what we know about the Vanderpump Rules cast salary.

Vanderpump Rules is Bravo’s reality TV series following the personal and professional lives of the staff at Real Housewives of Beverly Hills alum Lisa Vanderpump’s restaurant SUR Restaurant & Lounge in Los Angeles. The series—which premiered as a spin-off of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in 2013—started with original cast members Tom Sandoval, Jax Taylor, Kristen Doute, Stassi Schroeder, Katie Maloney, and Scheana Shay.

In 2023, Vanderpump Rules was nominated for its first Primetime Emmy Award in the Unstructured Reality Program category. The nomination came after Vanderpump Rules Season 10‘s shocking scandal involving cast members Ariana Madix, Tom Sandoval, and Raquel Leviss. The scandal — which has since been nicknamed #Scandoval — came after news broke that Tom and Raquel had a months-long affair while he was still dating and living with Ariana, whom he had been in a relationship with since 2014.

In an interview with Vanity Fair in January 2023, Ariana opened up about returning to Vanderpump Rules Season 11 following #Scandoval. “It’s going to be interesting because it is very much a time capsule. It really is going back in time, and it really is me in a very different place than I’m at right now,” she said. “There’s good days and there’s bad days. There’s ups and there’s downs. There’s definitely going to be those moments—the dancing, having-fun feeling like, I got this. And then there’s going to be moments where maybe I don’t got this. But that’s the journey I was on, and I feel like it’s just my job to let people in on that. So you’re going to see all of it.”

How much does the Vanderpump Rules cast make?

After more than a decade on television, a fair amount of Vanderpump Rules stars, also known as SURvers, have come and gone from the series, including original cast members like Jax, Kristen, and Stassi. So what is the Vanderpump Rules’ cast salary and how much do cast members like Lisa Vanderpump, Ariana Madix, and Tom Sandoval make? Read on for what we know about how much the Vanderpump Rules cast makes and their salaries per episode on Bravo’s Emmy-nominated reality show.

  • How much does Lisa Vanderpump make from Vanderpump Rules?

    Salary: More than $50,000 per episode

    Lisa Vanderpump is an executive producer and main cast member on Vanderpump Rules. She’s starred on the series since Season 1. Lisa—who previously starred on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills from Seasons 1 to 9—executive produces Vanderpump Rules with her husband, Ken Todd. While Lisa’s current Vanderpump Rules salary isn’t known, The Hollywood Reporter reported in 2020 that Lisa made more than $50,000 per episode at the time as an executive producer and cast member on Vanderpump Rules, along with “lucrative incentives.” The salary would pay Lisa around $1 million for a 20-episode season. The magazine also reported that Lisa’s salary was more than double what Vanderpump Rules‘ main cast members made, which was $25,000 per episode at the time, making her the highest-paid Vanderpump Rules cast member.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Lisa made $500,000 per season on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills at the time of her exit. Celebrity Net Worth also lists her net worth as $90 million.
