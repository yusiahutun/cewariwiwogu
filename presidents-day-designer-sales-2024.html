11 Best Presidents' Day Designer Sales in 2024

March 2024 · 4 minute read

We’re officially at that point of the season when warmer weather is on the horizon—and couldn’t come soon enough. The seemingly dismal month of February does have its merits, though, and now that Presidents’ Day sales and the three-day holiday weekend are finally here, why not use this opportunity to indulge in a little retail therapy?

Ahead, we combed through the virtual catalogues of our go-to luxury retailers—like Bergdorf Goodman, Saks Fifth Avenue, and Farfetch—to pull the best Presidents’ Day designer deals to help lift your spirits as spring approaches. With up to 80 percent off on items from quiet luxury staples by the Row to ethereal L’Agence dresses, this is your chance to save big on all the pieces you’ve been coveting for ages.

Bergdorf Goodman

Bergdorf Goodman is upping the ante with new pieces up to 70 percent off in its Designer Sale—and as extra incentive, from now through February 20, you can earn a gift card for as much as $12,000, depending on how much you spend, with code BGFEB. We’re currently eyeing structured pieces, like this menswear-inspired blazer and cashmere-blend sweater vest, that make for polished office attire.

Bloomingdale’s

Take up to 60 percent off a wide assortment of apparel, shoes, bags, and jewelry during Bloomingdale’s big womenswear sale (plus an extra 30 percent on certain pieces until February 19). For even lower prices, sift through the retailer’s final sale section to get 70 percent off a range of brands like Oscar de la Renta, Versace, and Lanvin.

Farfetch

Farfetch's current sale includes thousands of finds up to 90 percent off, from popular labels such as Ganni, Mugler, Jimmy Choo, and Khaite. Visit this sale for that one statement piece you can dress up or down, like a tartan skirt or slinky two-tone gown.

Matches Fashion

Don’t sleep on the Matches Fashion Outlet, which currently features names like Loewe, Staud, and Erdem at up to 80 percent off. Highlights include deeply discounted sundresses, handbags, and breezy tops to help you wow on your upcoming travels.

Moda Operandi

Because Moda Operandi was founded by former fashion editor Lauren Santo Domingo, everything on the site is curated with an expert eye. And right now, you can score a plethora of trendy finds from the likes of Chloé, Brandon Maxwell, the Row, and more for up to 70 percent off—plus, take an extra 20 percent off sale with code FEB20.

Mytheresa

Mytheresa currently has up to 70 percent off everything from jewelry to evening gowns. Try your hand at prep, maybe, with Marni’s cashmere color-blocked cardigan—our idea of the perfect transitional piece to carry you into spring.

Neiman Marcus

For a limited time, take up to 40 percent off new sale items from brands like Loro Piana, A.L.C., Carolina Herrera, and Etro. (You can also earn a gift card for up to $1,500 depending on how much you spend; use code PRESGC!) And if minimalism is your thing, consider some gorgeous streamlined silhouettes—like an inky-blue maxi skirt or structured strapless top—from Fabiana Filippi and Tove.

Net-a-Porter

Head to Net-a-Porter for an extra 25 percent off names like Jacquemus, Isabel Marant, Dries Van Noten, and Mara Hoffman when you use code EXTRA25. There are over 18,000 sale pieces to peruse, with highlights including flouncy dresses and well-tailored outerwear that will become staples in your closet.

Nordstrom

Nordstrom’s Winter Sale is happening now through February 23. You’ll find designer goods from brands like Balenciaga and Max Mara up to 50 percent off, with tons of sculptural bags scattered throughout.

Saks Fifth Avenue

Until February 17, Saks Fifth Avenue is offering gift cards of up to $500 with code MYGCSF, on a sliding scale depending on how much you buy. Separately, as part of the store’s designer sale, over 26,000 styles are currently up to 70 percent off. The current promotion includes deals not only on apparel and accessories, but on home and beauty products, too, so you can treat yourself and pick up a gift for anyone on your list. Might we suggest a new silk dress for date night or some stylish new luggage?

Shopbop

Besides a host of favorites like Jil Sander, Cecilie Bahnsen, and Christopher Esber, Shopbop is home to an impressive array of smaller designers and more accessible labels you’ll want on your radar. Right now, you can shop all of the above for up to 70 percent off in its sale section. If you’re looking to score a trendy, unique piece for less, this is the place to do it.

Elly Leavitt is a fashion and luxury commerce editor at Hearst covering fashion, design, and more across multiple brands. You can probably find her in line at Trader Joe's. You will never find her at SoulCycle.
