One of these 3.8million luxury villas in Fiji could be yours for 15!

January 2024 · 2 minute read

The villas will be custom designed and have their own swimming pool

    For anyone who's ever dreamed of owning a little slice of paradise, now's your chance. A new raffle is offering the chance to win one of five luxury villas on Fiji's beautiful Wavi Island – and it only costs £15 a ticket.

    Each winner will secure an incredible package valued at £3.84million, which not only includes a private three-bedroom villa with swimming pool, but also two five-night trips out to the island to see their custom property being built. And once the villas are built, the new owners will have access to their own boat mooring, use of the resort facilities and no management costs or fees until rental income is produced.

    One of these luxury villas could be yours for £15

    The indulgent experiences don't end there; winners will also have access to private chefs and staff so they never have to lift a finger. Sounds like a dream come true, right? However the only downside is that guests from the UK are in for a very long journey. It typically takes around 27 hours to fly from London to Fiji's international airport, Nadi, and then there is an additional one hour flight down to Savusavu's airport – a 20-minute drive away from Wavi Island.

    Each prize package is worth an estimated £3.8million

    Unspoiled and tranquil, Wavi Island is nestled off the shore of Vanua Levu and is accessible via private bridge. The villa owners will be able to spend their time relaxing and exploring the island, which spans 27 acres of lush tropical landscaping and is lined with pristine white sand beaches. But if they feel the need to venture further afield, some world-famous resorts are only 20 minutes away – including the five-star Namale resort.
