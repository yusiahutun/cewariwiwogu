See Chrissy Teigen's Sheer Leopard-Print Dress

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Chrissy Teigen is taking a walk on the wild side with her latest look. On Wednesday, the cookbook author attended City Harvest’s 2024 Gala: Magic of Motown in New York City alongside her husband, John Legend, repping sheer style with an on-trend animal print. 

For the occasion, Teigen left her bra at home and a wore a see-through slip dress with nothing but a black thong underneath. The nakedness of her gown, which was completely covered in cheetah spots, was offset by two coquettish black velvet ribbons that were fastened into bows at the shoulders and cascaded down past her waist. She accessorized with stacks of chunky gold bangles on both wrists, a black clutch, strappy sandals, and a pair of sculptural earrings. 

Further driving home the coquette girl aesthetic, Teigen pulled her brunette hair back into a bouncy high ponytail with face-framing pieces with flipped ends. Meanwhile, the rest of her glam leaned more sultry, consisting of a glossy nude lip, smoky eyes, and dark lacquered nails. 

Legend, for his part, expertly coordinated with his wife, opting for a black suit with sparkly brown embellishments on his jacket. 

At the event, the couple, who are parents to four children — Luna, Miles, Wren, and Esti — were asked if they wanted to try for a fifth baby. And while Teigen told reporters that she wanted another child after after "Wren started walking," Legend answered with a firm "no."

Page Six then questioned the musician about their household dynamics now with two new babies, to which he replied: "Oh, it’s so good. It’s just so much love in the house.” Hilariously, Teigen saw this as an opportunity to persuade Legend to have another, telling him, “And that’s what it would be like if we have a fifth!”
