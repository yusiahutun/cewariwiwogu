Best heart touching thank you message for your best friend

The best way to appreciate your best friend is through a touchy message. But finding the right words can be tricky as explaining your emotions on paper is hard for many people.

The great thing is that you have numerous message examples that you can send to your friend. Choose the most appropriate appreciation message for a friend to make them feel special.

  • Thank you for being there for me and making me feel supported, loved, and cared for.
  • Thanks for always cheering me up whenever life knocks me down. Thank you so much for being my friend!
  • I never feel lonely because of you guys. You guys always give me a place to belong. Thank you, my friends.
  • You are that person with whom I can share my tears and fears without hesitation. Thanks, best friend!
  • I have been fortunate to come in contact with people like you. You, my friend, are magnificent and wonderful. Thank you for everything you've done for me.
  • Having someone who makes you laugh and cheers you up when you’re down is a great blessing. Thanks, my friend, for being that someone in my life.
  • Thank you for helping me last week. It means a lot to me, and I couldn’t do anything without your help. I’m taking you to dinner with wine this weekend, and don’t you even try to say no!
  • Being your friend is such a blessing. You help me to cope with my problems, and you've been there for me through my ups and downs. You are the definition of ride or die. Thank you for being my guardian angel.
  • I feel so lucky to have you as a best friend. I have never met anyone this loving and supportive. I don’t say this often, but I’m grateful for everything you do for me. I love you, bud.
  • I am still trying to figure out how to thank you for what you did for me. Nobody has ever done so much to make me smile. That's how I know that you're my soulmate. Thank you for always being around, mate.
  • There aren't any right words in my vocabulary that will help me to describe what you mean to me, my friend. Thank you for always being there for me.
  • Being in a friendship with you means celebration to me every day. Thanks a lot for adding so many good memories and times of happiness to my life.
  • Even though situations, times and life have changed over the years, our friendship is still the same. Thanks for being such a sweet friend of mine.
  • I appreciate your love, kindness, and support! Thanks for being a part of my life, my friend. Thank you for your wishes.
  • You are the brightest star that fades away from all the darkness around me. Thanks for everything you do!

Long emotional thank you message to a friend

Few words are good. They are straightforward and without much fanfare. But many might feel deprived of expression, and the best way to give it your all is to compose something long.

And that is why a long emotional thank you message is a good idea. The message will have the proper context and communicate the appropriate message.

  • Some friends watch us when we cry and wish we’ll stop crying. Some friends want to do all they can, so we may stop crying. You’re none of these. You go the extra mile to put a smile on my face. Thank you for being such a fantastic friend.
  • There is so much I want to tell you about your gift! The first thing was how excited I felt when I opened the box. It was wrapped beautifully, and the note on top said, “Open Me First”. As I opened the card, I read a sweet message. Then, holding the velvety present in my hands, I felt so happy. A little box fell out as I lifted the wrapping paper! Inside was a bracelet with a beautiful charm symbolising our friendship: a tree.
  • As I made friends, I sought someone who loved the things I loved. Someone who would love my hobbies so that we could always be together. You came into my life being so different from me that I thought we wouldn’t click, but you taught me that friendship is not about being similar but respecting our differences and embracing variety. Thank you so much.
  • Meeting you brought me into a world I might not have been exposed to if I had not met you. A better me emerges from the former me because you came into my life. Thank you, friend.
  • Thank you for being brave and following your big dreams. It saddens me that I won’t be at your side as you take on this new endeavour, but rest assured that I am always here to support you when you need it. I am proud of who you are and the woman you have become. Remember how much I love you.
  • I thought about our imminent death in the cold trailer with my kids every night. I never thought about a stranger coming to our rescue. Thank you for saving our lives and seeking me to be your friend when no one else cared.
  • What could I have done without you on those days and nights when I thought my life was over and tears couldn’t dry from my eyes? You stood by my side and told me all those stupid jokes. You didn’t leave my side even when I said I didn’t want to see anyone. Thank you so much, my buddy. You are, indeed, an angel.
