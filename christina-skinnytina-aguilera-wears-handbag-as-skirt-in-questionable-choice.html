Christina 'Skinnytina' Aguilera wears 'handbag' as skirt in questionable choice

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Christina Aguilera has caught the attention of the internet as she wears her blinged-out pink Birkin bag as a skirt. Yes, you read that right, a skirt.

The music icon has been on the scene for over two decades and has been slaying the game ever since. We take a look at what fans have been saying about her new look as they hailed the singing legend ‘Skinnytina’.

Christina Aguilera shows off her ‘Birkin bag’ skirt

The Genie in a Bottle star stunned her 9.1 million Instagram followers this week as instead of carrying her blinged-out pink Birkin bag in her hand, she decided to wear it, as a skirt.

Christina was in her Barbie era with her bleach blonde hair, as she paired the look with a chic pair of shades and a baby black top, paired with some nude killer heels.

In the next slides, the star shared videos from the brand Namilla, where the bag-turned-skirt comes from. However, as well as just a skirt, this model also had a matching Birkin tube top. Slay!

Fans call the look ‘gorgeous’

Of course, fans rushed to the comments to compliment Christina’s outfit choice, and honestly, we’re living for it!

Others were complimenting the star on her killer body as her mini-skirt showed off her toned legs.

“She’s in her bag,” wrote one, to which another replied: “Quite literally, lol.”

“THE ORIGINAL SKINNY LEGEND OMG” exclaimed another.

“SKINNYTINA IS IN THE HOUSE,” wrote one.

Another exclaimed: “SO GORGEOUS”

“Looking like a bag of money!!” wrote another.

Christina attends Selena Gomez’s birthday party

A couple of weeks ago, Selena Gomez managed to get an adorable shot with the star. She hosted an A-List birthday bash attended many celebrity faces including Paris Hilton and Christina herself.

The 42-year-old posted a picture of herself and the newly turned 31-year-old’s party which she captioned: “Love these girls,” tagging Gomez and Karol G.

Selena’s dress was the one standing out this time, as the birthday girl should, as she stunned in a red rose mini dress.
