19 of the best modern oud perfumes

April 2024 · 5 minute read

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Les Eaux Primordiales Oud Superfluid EDP

Les Eaux Primordiales Oud Superfluid EDP

Imposing black bottle aside, Oud Superfluide is pleasantly wearable, almost as though the note is embracing the softer side of things, all wrapped up in a fluffy cardigan. It opens with a zing of orange before drying to a gentle hug on the skin.

BDK Parfums 312 Saint-Honoré EDP

BDK Parfums 312 Saint-Honoré EDP

The longevity of oud meets the pillowy, airy sweetness of marshmallows and powdered sugar. This is an oud scent for the brighter months, to be worn with lacy white dresses. Composed with creamy musks and iris, 312 Saint-Honoré proves that there really is an oud for every season.

Initio Paragon Extrait de Parfum, £255

Initio Paragon Extrait de Parfum, £255

The words calming and serene are not usually associated with the opulence of oud, but when balanced with cool lavender and herbaceous sage, Paragon manages to transform from a head-clearing first spritz to a comforting waft of scent as the day goes on. It's an extrait, so expect impressive longevity.

Memoize London Industria Extrait de Parfum

Memoize London Industria Extrait de Parfum

A joyful blend of bountiful florals paired with effervescent citrus fruits, perfect for high summer.

Laboratorio Olfattivo ExpLOud Eau de Parfum

Laboratorio Olfattivo ExpLOud Eau de Parfum

For an easy-to-wear, everyday oud scent that's friendly even in small spaces, look no further. There are no hard edges here, just a smooth composition of Indian oud flanked by soft gardenia and lily of the valley while watery notes lend an airy freshness to the overall scent.

Byredo Rouge Chaotique

Byredo Rouge Chaotique

A disco-ready scent that opens with glitzy, glossy blackcurrants with a syrupy, succulent plum undertone. The subtlest hint of leather and patchouli adds depth to this fruity take on oud. Take extra care when spritzing on lighter coloured clothes as the tinted formula might cause staining.

Granado Oud Eau de Cologne

Granado Oud Eau de Cologne

For an airy splash of oud, try Granado's sheer and lightweight interpretation of the opulent scent. More 'fragranced water' than a heavy perfume, this is one to keep in your bathroom shelf for daily wear.

Kilian Rose Oud EDP

Kilian Rose Oud EDP

A femme fatale oud with generous doses of shimmering rose petals, laced with juicy lychee. It's a glittering party dress of a scent, with a sprinkling of cedar and saffron to balance out the sweetness.

Veronique Gabai Oud Elixir EDP

Veronique Gabai Oud Elixir EDP

If it's a sensational, showstopping, ballgown of a fragrance you're after, just a spritz (or two, if you're feeling particularly audacious) of Oud Elixir will do the job. It's a takes-no-prisoners cocktail of oud and warm cardamom that takes life by the reigns; bold, conspicuous and confidence-boosting.

Hermès Oud Alezan Eau de Parfum

Hermès Oud Alezan Eau de Parfum

Christine Nagel's latest creation for the house of Hermès is a note-perfect ode to a classic olfactory pairing: oud and rose. This bold, spicy blend opens with a modern, just-sweet-enough floral note, before unveiling the traditional spicy warmth beneath. If you're new to oud and want something seductive yet wearable, this is a flawless choice.

LilaNur Parfums Agar Épicé EDP

LilaNur Parfums Agar Épicé EDP

An energising dose of ginger lifts the richness of oud before settling into a powdery, woody concoction. One to spritz on your jumper for lingering cosiness.

Molton Brown Rose Dunes EDP

Molton Brown Rose Dunes EDP

Inspired by sand dunes at golden hour, a sprinkling of saffron adds warmth to sumptuous rose petals and earthy patchouli.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Art de Parfum Sensual Oud Extrait de Parfum

Art de Parfum Sensual Oud Extrait de Parfum

A quiet luxury take on oud, this polite blend of fluttery, soft pink rose petals and green geranium leaves is pleasantly wearable for daytime and those who are easing into ouds.

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Oud Satin Mood EDP

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Oud Satin Mood EDP

Baccarat Rouge 540 may be the current fan favourite, but don't sleep on Maison Francis Kurkdjian's Oud Satin Mood. Rather than a full blast of cloying oud, there's a note of creamy, vanilla-drizzled rose here that'll keep you coming back for more. Just like BR450, this has excellent sillage so get ready for the compliments to roll in.

Jo Malone London Oud & Bergamot Cologne Intense

Jo Malone London Oud & Bergamot Cologne Intense

Jo Malone London shines at creating sunlit, summertime fragrances and Oud & Bergamot is no exception. It sees the richness of oud shot through with freshly squeezed citrus. It's clean, fresh, and perfect all year round.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Juliette Has A Gun Another Oud EDP

Juliette Has A Gun Another Oud EDP

Hot pink, red berries, sparkling sequins...if any of these resonate with you, then Another Oud is your poison. It's fizzing with charm and is instantly cheering thanks to hints of raspberries, bergamot and a base of fluffy, sweet musk.

Cartier Oud & Ambre EDP

Cartier Oud & Ambre EDP

If it's drama and decadence you're after, a spritz of Cartier's Oud & Ambre should do it. This opulent scent is oud at its most refined; buttery smooth and with plenty of depth without ever being heady. Hosting a dinner party? This will definitely stir conversations and the impressive longevity will have you dazzling all night.

Dior Oud Ispahan EDP

Dior Oud Ispahan EDP

An unapologetic, roaring oud laced with velvety rose petals and a cloud of incense. Not one for the faint of heart, but it's guaranteed to turn heads in the best way.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

Akro Night EDP

Akro Night EDP

From the perfumer that brought us icons such as Mugler Angel and Dolce & Gabbana Light Blue comes Akro, an ode to moments of pleasure and debauchery. Night conjures up memories of an evening out with cocktails aplenty, a potential romance just brewing around the corner. Under the masterful nose of Olivier Cresp, this translates into a sophisticated composition of rose, powdery iris and just a touch of oud.
