Geordie Shore star emotional over rift with Chloe Ferry after dad's tragic death

Geordie Shore star Bethan Kershaw has expressed her doubts regarding the upcoming reunion saying "it's the last thing she wants" while also discussing her fractured friendship with Chloe Ferry.

While appearing on the new series of the popular MTV show, Bethan spoke openly to Nathan Henry about meeting up with other members of the cast.

But as she reflected on her relationships from the show, Bethan also revealed that she hadn't spoken to best friend Chloe for five months and felt "so sad" about their rift.

Discussing Chloe's recent heartache after losing her father, Bethan said she feels Chloe no longer confides in her.

Sharing her thoughts in the confessional, a heart-broken Bethan said: "I just hate talking about it because it's a really s**t situation and I just feel so bad."

"But like, I just don't want us to go back to where we were.

"I just wish she could just talk to us so we could sort it out. Like I've tried to reach out to her so many times."

Later on in the programme, Chloe met up with her pal Marty McKenna where she also discussed her fractured relationship with Bethan and revealed that she doesn't feel Bethan was there for her during her toughest ordeal.

Speaking in her confessional, an upset Chloe said: "Sadly last year my dad passed away and I just feel like Bethan wasn't there for us.

"When you feel that sort of pain, you look at life totally totally different and I needed my best friend."

She added: "I kept saying to her 'I'm struggling, I'm struggling' and every time I talked to her she never asked us how I am.

"What's made it more annoying is the fact that she's gone round to people instead of trying to reach out to us."

Chloe later shared that she may still be able to be friends with Bethan but thinks they may not be as close as they once were.

Although there was a lot of drama going in the first episode of the popular MTV show, fans were glad the new series was back on screens.

One person wrote: "It’s been around 6 years since I last watched @mtvgeordieshore as Charlotte left the show but now she is back!!!! I don’t think I can express the excitement I have to see @Charlottegshore and the other cast members come back together tonight! #GeordieShore #mtv."
