Demi Bagby is a professional athlete, fitness model, and social media influencer from San Diego, California. She is known for her impressive acrobatics and weightlifting skills, which she showcases on her social media platforms. Here is a detailed biography timeline of her life and career.

  • 2001: Demi Bagby was born on January 10 in San Diego, California.
  • 2014: Bagby suffers a serious cheerleading accident, breaking her back and causing her to be temporarily paralyzed.
  • 2015: Bagby begins her recovery and rehabilitation, discovering her love for weightlifting and fitness.
  • 2016: Bagby becomes a certified personal trainer and shares her fitness journey on social media.
  • 2017: Bagby gains popularity on social media for her impressive acrobatic and weightlifting skills, earning over a million followers on Instagram.
  • 2018: Bagby competes in her first fitness competition, the Wodapalooza Fitness Festival, placing 13th in the women’s division.
  • 2019: Bagby becomes a sponsored athlete for several fitness and lifestyle brands, including Reebok and Bang Energy.

  • 2021: Bagby expands her social media reach, gaining over 2.5 million followers on TikTok and launching her YouTube channel.
