Early years of Yua Mikami

Yua Mikami was born as Momona Kito on August 16, 1993, in Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture, Japan. As of 2020, she is 26 years old.  Ever since her childhood Yua set her mind to became a sucessful idol. At the age of thirteen, she did an audition for Morning Musume’s 8th generation in 2006. During that time, she was eliminated in the first round.

However, Yua didn’t give up on her dream and continue practicing. After years of hard-working finally, she made her debut in 2009 as a second-generation member of the idol group SKE48. During her career as a SKE48 member, she got into numerous setbacks, including a demotion to trainee status, as well as an underage dating and drinking scandal that broke out on July 2013. In 2014 she left the idol group and took a year gap from the entertainment industry.

After a year, she made a debut in an adult entertainment industry under the name of Muteki. Also, she made her debut as a member of the South Korean idol group called Honey Popcorn in 2018.
