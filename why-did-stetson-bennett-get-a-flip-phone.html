Why Did Stetson Bennett Get A Flip Phone -Was He Distracted By Smartphone?

Stetson Bennett switched to a flip phone after he worried he was wasting too much of his valuable time doing nothing but scrolling through feeds on his old smartphone. 

In a recent candid interview, he confessed that after he spent more than an hour on his phone, he could hardly remember what he spent those hours on.

He felt he was not doing anything important and realized he needed to focus on time management with schools and games taking much of his time. 

For most of us, it's hard to imagine carrying around a flip phone in today's age of technology, where smartphones have found a way to nestle in our hearts and our minds.

However, Stetson Bennett says he did it for himself to focus on important things after he found out he was being distracted away from them with his old phone.
