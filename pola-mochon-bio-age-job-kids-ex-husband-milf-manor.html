When you’re old and divorced, you lose hope of ever finding love again (especially if you prefer younger men). This Pola Mochon Bio explores her life.

Pola Mochon On MILF Manor

Never before! On MILF Manor, older women who also happen to be mothers are dating younger men — almost half their ages! However, the MILFS aren’t simply looking for a good time, but rather a true love who’d be with them when they are even older.

Our star, Pola Mochon was one of the featured eight women who were searching for attractive younger men at a tropical destination. However, unlike her co-stars who’ve regularly been in the dating game, she’s been out of it for nine long years. So, she’s got to work harder to charm her prince charming.

As per the network’s press release, “Although they may be a few years older than the average dating show contestant, these women are not out of practice. Ranging from 40 to 60 years young, this vibrant group brings their unique life experiences to the game.”

Joining Pola for the fun were her co-stars — Kelle (a real estate broker and wellness and fitness coach, and a mother of six), Shannan (an event planner for a health insurance company formally married for 16 years), SoYoung (a hairstylist turned surgical nurse who specializes in heart surgery), April Jayne (a “former heavy metal video vixen” who works as a personal trainer), Stefany Johnson (a real estate agent and a paralegal), Charlene (just come out of a toxic marriage), and April (an event planner and insurance rep with a strong work ethic and an even stronger personality).

MILF Manor premiered on January 15, 2023, and it indeed was interesting to watch this relatively unexplored concept in the reality dating world. Unlike other shows, MILF Manor didn’t even highlight the couples’ age gaps for everyone knew that the dating pool consisted of men half their age.
