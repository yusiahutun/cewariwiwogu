Who Is Michaiah Hanks? All About Chet Hanks Daughter With His Girlfriend

October 2024 · 7 minute read

Michaiah Hanks is the daughter of actor and musician Chet Marlon Hanks and Tiffany Myles. She is better known as the granddaughter of actor Tom Hanks. Her father, Chet Hanks, has starred in prominent roles in the TV series Empire and Shameless.

Chet’s daughter was not born into a serious relationship, but her father considers her the best thing that ever happened to him. He says that Mikey helped him change his life. At no point does he fail to mention how much his daughter means to him.

But who is Mishaya’s mother? How did she meet Chet Hanks? Were they just friends? And how much does Tom himself adore Michaiah? Find out below.

Michaiah’s mother, Tiffany Miles, was neither Chet’s girlfriend nor did they have any romantic relationship. From what I hear, they simply had a one-night stand when the celebrity’s son was drunk.

However, Chet claims that the woman who gave him his daughter is the nicest person he has ever met.

The heavily tattooed artist also paid a heartfelt tribute to his daughter’s black mother, Miles, during the George Floyd protests. Posting a series of Instagram photos showing him standing in front of George Floyd’s artwork, Chet wrote how grateful he was for Tiffany, calling her “the mother of my child and a beautiful black woman”.

Michaiah Hanks’ Father Is An Actor & Singer

Despite having a lot of experience with the screen, Chet Hanks hasn’t yet gained the huge fame that his father has over the decades. However, Michaiah’s father has had recurring roles in hits such as Empire and Shameless. He also starred as Joey Maldini in the Showtime legal drama miniseries Your Honor. Some of the films he has appeared in include Bratz, Indiana Jones and the Kingdom of the Crystal Skull, Larry Crowne, Project X, Fantastic Four, and Greyhouse.

In addition to acting, Chet is also a musician and rapper. In 2011, he recorded the song “White and Purple” under the pseudonym Chet Haze. The song was a remix of Wiz Khalifa’s single “Black and Yellow”.

In 2016, Chet formed the musical duo FTRZ with Drew Arthur. Two years later, they released two singles (“Models” and “NowhereLand”), as well as the EP “Ocean Park”. The duo then released two tracks in 2020, “Harley” and “Ticket Out My Head”, under the new name Something Out West.

Mikaya’s mother, Tiffany Miles, is a graduate of California State University. She also interns at Moguldom Media Group LLC. Miles works in the news industry in downtown Louth. She helps manage YouTube, Twitter, and email accounts. Her other jobs include running the teleprompter during live broadcasts, handling paperwork, and preparing equipment for red-carpet appearances.
