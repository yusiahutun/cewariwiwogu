101 Fashion Quotes So Timeless They're Basically Iconic

April 2024 · 11 minute read

If you’re a fashion-lover like myself, you’ve no doubt had at least one AIM away message, Instagram bio, or gallery wall piece referencing a quote by Coco Chanel, Karl Lagerfeld or some other fashion great. Great fashion quotes are almost as iconic as the designers themselves and the clothes they make, but if you’re getting sick of recycling the same tired few, we’ve taken the liberty of rounding up the 101 most legendary lines ever. I’d be willing to bet that even the most experienced fashionistas haven’t heard every single one!

Related Stories

While all these quotes are technically about fashion, they’re really about so much more than that. Fashion plays on life, on art, on love! When a designer is talking about a particularly moving gown or element of fashion, their words often relate to so much more. Some of the quotes on this list are short and cheeky, while others are more thoughtful and profound. Still, all are pretty much iconic. Some come from designers you might have hanging in your closet (Rachel Zoe, Marc Jacobs), others are haute couture greats (Christian Dior, Yves Saint Laurent) and a few are fictitious (Miranda Priestly, Carrie Bradshaw), though just as noteworthy as their IRL counterparts.

Sift through this list until you find sentiments worth coming back to, and add those to your collection of know-how. Will the renowned Diana Vreeland inspire you? Or will it be the great Christian Louboutin, the incomparable Alexander McQueen or someone else entirely? There’s only one way to find out—keep scrolling.

1. “You either know fashion or you don’t.” – Anna Wintour

2. “You can never take too much care over the choice of your shoes. Too many women think that they are unimportant, but the real proof of an elegant woman is what is on her feet.” – Christian Dior

3. “Fashion is what you’re offered four times a year by designers. And style is what you choose.” – Lauren Hutton

4. “Florals for spring? Groundbreaking.” – Miranda Priestly, The Devil Wears Prada

5. “I loathe narcissism, but I approve of vanity.” – Diana Vreeland

6. “One is never over-dressed or under-dressed with a Little Black Dress.” – Karl Lagerfeld

7. “The customer is the final filter. What survives the whole process is what people wear. I’m not interested in making clothes that end up in some dusty museum.” – Marc Jacobs

8. “I think there is beauty in everything. What ‘normal’ people would perceive as ugly, I can usually see something of beauty in it.” – Alexander McQueen

9. “Shoes transform your body language and attitude. They lift you physically and emotionally.” – Christian Louboutin

10. “I like my money right where I can see it: hanging in my closet.” – Carrie Bradshaw, Sex And The City

11. “She can beat me, but she cannot beat my outfit.” – Rihanna
