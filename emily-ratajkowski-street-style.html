Emily Ratajkowski's Street Style Is the 2024 Hot Girl Walk Uniform

Ever since the weather got better in New York, Emily Ratajkowski has been on the town. She’s been going on walks, hitting up the Japanese grocery store, and serving looks. Now that it’s warm enough for hot girl walks again, Emily Ratajkowski’s street style is the blueprint for the hot girl walk uniform update.

Today, she wore a cropped black and white baby tee with gray basketball shorts and a white baseball cap. She wore the red Puma sneakers she’s been wearing all spring. We know that the Adidas Sambas have been having the longest moment in the world, to the extent that the Prime Minister of the U.K. Rishi Sunak wore a pair and sent fashion heads into a spiral. So if you needed a Sambas alternative, the red Pumas are a great soccer trainer, and they’re Emrata-approved.

Her walk outfits seem to range from the last clothes left before laundry day to trousers and a blazer with a crop top underneath, and we should all be taking that note. My personal philosophy is if you’re the kind of person who serves a major look, you are going to have to have some random looking outfits sometimes. That’s how the big looks get workshopped. But from what we can tell, Emrata’s looks are all about switching up the bottoms.
