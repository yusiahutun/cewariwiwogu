Top 40 Country Songs June 2013

September 2024 · 3 minute read

The top hits on the Top 40 Country Songs for June 2013 list resemble May's collection, but there is a big change at the top. Darius Rucker's 'Wagon Wheel' held down the No. 1 spot then, but this monthm a different fun, summertime jam is the undisputed hottest song in country music...

A number of singles that had been simmering in the Top 20 or Top 30 made huge leaps in June, as well. Randy Houser's 'Runnin' Outta Moonlight' bounces to No. 10 (from No. 22), and Billy Currington's 'Hey Girl' shoots to No. 16 (from No. 33) -- both on the strength of strong digital sales.

Keith Urban's new single makes the biggest jump, however. 'Little Bit of Everything' wasn't even on the radar in May 2013, and now he sits inside the Top 10 for June. Scroll down to find your favorite songs.

The list of the Top 40 Country Songs for June 2013 is compiled from Billboard chart data and sales figures, in addition to staff opinion and reader feedback.
