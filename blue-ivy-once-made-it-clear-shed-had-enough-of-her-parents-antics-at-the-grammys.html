Blue Ivy once made it clear she'd had enough of her parent's antics at the Grammys

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Only Blue Ivy could tell her parents, Beyoncé and Jay-Z, to be quiet, and that’s exactly what she did at the 2018 Grammys. Her iconic moment has earned itself in showbiz history – and we’re here for it.

Beyoncé and Jay-Z have been together since the early 2000s before they tied the knot in 2008. The music stars then welcomed Blue Ivy Carter, 12, three years later. Then, two years after the release of Lemonade and the Jay-Z cheating scandal, they welcomed twins Rumi and Sir in 2017. But the following year, Blue proved deep down, the celebrity couple were just like all other parents being bossed about by their little ones.

Blue Ivy hilariously shushed Beyoncé and Jay-Z at the Grammys

At the Grammys in 2018, the camera caught the family of three sitting in the audience watching the ceremony. Beyoncé and Jay-Z can be seen clapping. However, even though her parents weren’t being particularly rowdy at the Oscars award show, six-year-old Blue Ivy didn’t seem to appreciate her parents being “too much.”

She was quick to turn to each of them to get their attention, and they could be seen leaning in to listen to her to hear what she wanted to say. Her hand gesture seems to suggest she wants them to be quieter as she gives them a serious look.

The celebrities were nominated for three awards at the 61st Grammys for their joint The Carters project Everything is Love. They picked up the award for Best Urban Contemporary Album. The album came to be two years after Beyoncé’s Lemonade, which deals with the theme of infidelity and Jay-Z’s 4:44 on which he admitted he was unfaithful to his wife.
