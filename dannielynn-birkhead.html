Dannielynn Birkhead Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth

September 2024 · 4 minute read

Dannielynn Hope Marshall Birkhead born Hannah Rose Marshall Stern is an American child model and reality television personality. she is famous for being the daughter of Anne Nicole Smith and Larry Birkhead.

Early life

Dannielynn Birkhead was born on September 7, 2006, in Nassau, The Bahamas to mother Anna Nicole Smith and father Larry Birkhead. Her mother was an American model, actress, and television personality who died cause of a drug overdose when Dannielynn was only 5 months old. Whereas her father, Larry is a photographer and television personality. She has a half-brother Daniel Wayne Smith who died due to an accidental overdose. When she was of 16 months, she underwent surgery on one of her eyes.

Dannielynn is currently attending high school in Kentucky and living with her father in their 10,00-square-foot Kentucky home. As a young teenager, she practices the violin, makes homemade slime, and spends time with her pet lizard.

Dannielynn Birkhead Career

When Dannielynn was of 6 years of age, she participated in a modeling campaign for Guess Kids which is the children’s line for Guess clothing. Afterward, the campaign was followed by the spring 2013 line of the same brand. This was the beginning of her modeling career. Then in 2007, she appeared on six episodes of Access Hollywood till 2012. Subsequently, she was featured on many entertainment news channels including Entertainment Tonight, True Crime with Aphrodite Jones for her mother’s documentary titled, ‘Anna Nicole Smith: Tragedy on Trial’. Due to Dannielynn’s involvement in a few high-profile entertainment cases, in the initial years, she did make the headline in many news channels.

Moreover, as her father is also a television personality, she featured on Celebrity Wife Swap in 2014. Subsequently, in 2015, Birkhead was seen on The Millionaire Matchmaker, ABC.com, Inside Edition, recently in 2018 again on Entertainment Tonight and one appearance on Steve Harvey. Recently in February 2021, she was seen on the reality television show 20/20 where she explored the life and legacy her mother, Anne Nicole Smith left behind. There are no details regarding Birkhead’s upcoming projects, she currently seems to be focused on her academics and school, and has even made the Honor Roll.

Controversies

Dannielynn Birkhead Paternity Test

Dannielynn was named Hannah Rose Marshall Stern at birth as she was born to Anna Nicole Smith and Howard K. Stern on the birth certificate. After Dannielynn’s half-brother Daniel Smith’s death which was quickly followed three days after her birth, suspicion arose about Dannielynn’s parents. Paternity claims were made by Howard K. Stern, Larry Birkhead, Alexander Denk, and Frederic von Anhalt. After the DNA test, it was established that Dannielynn is the daughter of Birkhead, and after getting custody her name was changed from Stern to Birkhead.
