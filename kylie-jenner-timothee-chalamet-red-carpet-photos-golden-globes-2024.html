Kylie Jenner Wore a Sheer Lace Dress with Timothe at the 2024 Golden Globes

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet are red carpet official!

The cosmetics mogul made a surprise appearance at the Golden Globes tonight to support her man. The two entered L.A.’s Beverly Hilton hotel in coordinating all-black ensembles.

Jenner looked striking in a sheer, lace bodycon dress with a low back, embellished with shiny black beads; it was a vintage piece by Hanae Mori. Her only accessories seemed to be a diamond ear cuff and Jimmy Choo's black “Bonny” clutch. She wore her signature bombshell makeup and her hair was up in a messy, chic bun, with loose, wavy strands framing her face. Chalamet, meanwhile, was dapper in a sleek black textured suit embellished with oh-so many sequins, by Celine Homme. He accessorized with a black-and-silver chain necklace and silver-and-black diamond-encrusted watch, both by Cartier.

Christopher Polk/Golden Globes 2024//Getty Images

The two did not walk the red carpet together, but were seen entering the ceremony hand in hand—and later, kissing.

Jenner and Chalamet began dating early last year, and made their first public appearance together in September, when they attended Beyoncé’s star-studded birthday concert and then packed on the PDA at the U.S. Open men’s finals.

Chalamet is nominated for Best Actor in a Musical or Comedy Film tonight for his lead role in Wonka. Released late last year, the musical movie tells the tale of young chocolatier Willy Wonka as he works to make his dream of opening the best chocolate store in Paris a reality. Chalamet has been widely praised for his charming performance in the film, which sees him act, dance, and sing.

In the category, Chalamet is up against Nicolas Cage (Dream Scenario), Matt Damon (Air), Paul Giamatti (The Holdovers), Joaquin Phoenix (Beau Is Afraid), and Jeffrey Wright (American Fiction).
