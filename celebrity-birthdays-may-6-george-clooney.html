Celebrity birthdays 6 May: George Clooney, Jamie Jewitt, and Naomi Scott

September 2024 Â· 2 minute read

A special happy birthday to these famous faces!

    A warm happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating today (6 May). Here at HELLO!, we are taking a look at all the famous faces who will be toasting their birthdays with their loved ones. From a Hollywood actor to a rising star and a very well known Love Islander, take a look below to see who is celebrating today:

    George Clooney turns 57

    Happy birthday to this silver fox! George Clooney will be turning 57. The A-list star is no doubt celebrating his birthday in New York with his wife Amal Clooney. On the day after his birthday he'll be hitting the glamorous red carpet for the 2018 Met Gala. No rest for the wicked.

    Jamie Jewitt turns 28

    This time last year we didn't know who Jamie Jewitt was - but then Love Island 2017 happened, and the rest is history. The former Calvin Klein model found love on the ITV show with Camilla Thurlow, and they are still very much loved up now. The gorgeous Essex boy will no doubt be celebrating his big day with his girlfriend and his family. The pair often post photos on Instagram mingling with one another's families. 

    Naomi Scott turns 25

    If you don't know Naomi Scott, you will do soon! The British singer and actress was spotted by Eternal's Kelle Bryan, and has now signed up to star alongside Will Smith in the live adaptation of . She also recently starred in the movie as the pink Power Ranger. This girl is definitely one to watch! For her 25th birthday she'll no doubt be gearing up for her first UK tour which is taking place from 22 May until 31 May.
