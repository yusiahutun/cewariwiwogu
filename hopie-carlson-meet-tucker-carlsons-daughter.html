Hopie Carlson: Meet Tucker Carlson's Daughter

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Since her birth, Hopie Carlson has been in the media. It’s not surprising since her father, Tucker Carlson, is one of the most famous reporters in the United States. Tucker Carlson is an American political commentator, television personality, and writer. On Fox News, he is popular as the former host of the nightly political show, “Tucker Carlson Tonight.”

Despite being one of the most successful TV personalities, Tucker keeps details regarding his family private. Consequently, fans are curious about his family, especially his grown-up daughter, Hopie.

What is she up to these days? How many siblings does she have? Is she married? Here’s everything you need to know about the celebrity child.

Hopie Carlson’s Background

Hopie was born in 1999 in Virginia, United States, to Tucker Carlson and his wife, Susan Andrews. However, like most details about her early life, her birthdate is unknown. Hopie grew up with her parents in their family’s farmhouse in Alexandria, Virginia. She attended St. Patrick’s Episcopal Day School for her elementary education.

After graduating from elementary school, Hopie Carlson enrolled at St. George’s School, Rhodes Island, where her parents met. While in High school, Hopie developed an interest in sporting activities. She participated in swimming and other athletics. Consequently, she took the mantle of senior prefect. 

After high school, she enrolled at Virginia University. However, her course of study is kept private. 

How Did Hopie Carlson’s Parents Meet?

Hopie Carlson’s parents, Tucker Carlson and Susan Andrews met in high school as students at St. George’s School in Rhode Island. At the time, Hopie’s maternal grandfather was the headmaster. According to the famous tv personality, “Andrews was the cutest 10th grader in America.”

The then-teenagers fell head over heels in love and dated exclusively for years. The pair maintained their relationship after they parted ways to attend college. Eventually, the lovebirds got engaged six months before he graduated from Trinity College in 1991. After which, they tied the knot in a private wedding ceremony at their high school chapel.

How Many Siblings Does Hopie Carlson Have?

Like Hopie, there is little information about her siblings. However, we can confirm that the celebrity child has three siblings. Together, Tucker and Andrews have welcomed four kids, including Hopie.

First is their daughter, Lillie Carlson, who was born in 1994. She is the oldest of the Carlson siblings. Like Hopie, she attended St George’s School in Rhode Island. After graduating from high school, she enrolled at the university. Aside from her educational achievements, there are no details regarding her private life. 

Next is Buckley Carlson, born in 1997, and he is the second child and only son of the couple. Like Hopie, he also attended the University of Virginia, where he was a Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity member. Buckley works as a Communications Director for US Congressman Jim Banks. Additionally, he is a popular name in the political circle, like his father.

Then comes Dorothy Carlson, the youngest child of the famous couple. According to reports, Dorothy was born in 2002 and is inactive on social media. Like the rest of her siblings, details about her life are shrouded in secrecy. Hence, there is little to no information about her.

Is Hopie Carlson Married?

While Hopie Carlson has not revealed any details about her love life or any partners, her father’s fans wonder if she secretly tied the knot. Along with most details about her life, her relationship status is kept stringently private. However, because she is in college, we can confirm she has not tied the knot yet. 

Like her siblings, Hopie Carlson has not appeared in public with anyone or opened up about dating anyone. Her father’s fans, however, believe this has to do with his comment during one of his segments in September 2019. The TV personality revealed he would never allow his daughters to go out with a feminist. 

Tucker added that he thinks people who identify as feminists are “a creep.” This assertion received backlash from fans who believed his belief system was faulty.

What Is Hopie Carlson’s Net Worth?

Hopie has no verifiable career. Hence, there are no details about her net worth. However, her father has a net worth of $30,000,000, which he amassed from his lengthy career as a political commentator, author, and TV personality. 

Fans can’t wait to see what the next few years hold for the celebrity child. Will she pursue an entertainment career or become a political commentator like her father? Whatever career path she chooses, she has her parents to guide her.
