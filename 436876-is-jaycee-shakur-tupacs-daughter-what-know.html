What were Tupac's last words?

According to a now retired cop Chris Caroll, Tupac's last word were "F*ck you". Carroll disclosed that prior to 2Pac being transported to the hospital, he had partial responsiveness and a hint of confrontational behavior. He stated:

He went from struggling to speak, being noncooperative, to an ‘I’m at peace’ type of thing, just like that. He looked at me, and he took a breath to get the words out, and he opened his mouth, and I thought I was actually going to get some cooperation. And then the words came out: ‘F**k you.’ After that, he started gurgling and slipping out of consciousness.

Shakur slipped into a coma, and after six days, he ultimately passed away, officially pronounced dead on September 13th 1996.
