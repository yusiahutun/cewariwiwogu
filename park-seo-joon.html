Table of Biography

Park Seo-Joon is a famous South Korean actor. Park Seo-Joon is also a singer and model. He is famous for his roles in dramas and TV shows including “The Chronicles of Evil”, “Itaewon Class”, “Why Secretary Kim”, “Fight for My Way”, “Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth”, and “She Was Pretty” among others.

Early Life

Park Seo-Joon was born on December 16, 1988, and he is currently 35 years old. He hails from Seoul, South Korea, and his zodiac sign is Sagittarius.

Further, his birth name is Park Yong-kyu, written in Korean as 박용규. his name is written in Korean as 박서준 and in Hanja as 朴敘俊.

There is no information regarding his parents, siblings, and other family details. However, there is information that he has two younger brothers.

Regarding his educational journey, he did his alma mater the Seoul Institute of the Arts. He also graduated from Kyungpook National University.

Career and Professional Life

ParkSeo-Joonn is a prominent actor, model, and singer. The actor is most popular for his roles in TV dramas including Kill Me, Heal Me, She Was Pretty,

Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth, Fight for My Way, What’s Wrong with Secretary Kim, and Itaewon Class among others. Seo-Joonn is also a part of films like Chronicles of Evil, Midnight Runners, and The Divine Fury.

He was signed under the entertainment company “Content Y” until June 2018. After his contract expired, Seo-joon then signed with “Awesome Ent” in July 2018.

Awesome Ent is a company established by Seo-joon’s longtime manager.

The actor made his entertainment debut in the year 2011. He appeared in the music video for Bang Yong-guk’s single I Remember.

Following this, he appeared in TV dramas Dream High 2 in the year 2012, Pots of Gold in 2013, One Warm Word in 2013, and A Witch’s Love in 2014.

Park Seo-Joon – Rise to Popularity

Park’s breakout role came in the year 2015. It was with the dramas Kill Me, Heal Me and She Was Pretty. For both these projects, he worked alongside actress Hwang Jung-eum in the lead roles.

The same year, Seo-Joon also featured in the thriller film The Chronicles of Evil. In the year 2016, he starred in the youth historical drama Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth.

 For this project, Seo-joon worked alongside Go Ara and Park Hyung-sik. Other actors in the lead roles in this project were Choi Min-ho, Do Ji-han, Jo Yoon-woo, and Kim Tae-hyung among others.

Additionally, he found immense success in the 2017 youth romance drama Fight for My Way. He worked with actress Kim Ji-won in the lead roles. Likewise, the TV series was a major hit in South Korea and topped ratings in its time slot.

The same year, Seo-Joon played his first big-screen leading role in the action-comedy Midnight Runners.

He worked alongside actor Kang Ha-neul for this project. Seo-Joon also won the “Best New Actor” award at major film awards ceremonies like the Grand Bell Awards and the Korean Association of Film Critics Awards for this role.

Caption: Park Seo-joon appearing on the poster of the film ‘Midnight Runners’ alongside co-star Kang Ha-neul. Source: Lotte Entertainment

2018 Onwards

Similarly, in tvN’s 2018 romantic-comedy-drama What’s Wrong with Secretary Kim, actor Seo-joon starred alongside the prominent actress Park Min-young.

This series was a hit and Seo-joon received positive reviews. For his performance in this drama, the Korean press dubbed Seo-joon the “master of the romantic comedy”.

Then in 2019, the actor starred in the occult film The Divine Fury and also served as the special juror for the Asiana International Short Film Festival.

Seo-Joon also made a guest appearance in the Oscar-winning movie Parasite the same year.

In the year 2020, Park Seo-Joon starred in JTBC’s drama Itaewon Class. His role in this drama was that of a restaurant owner. This drama is based on the webtoon of the same name.

The actor is also set to appear in the sports film Dream. His role in this film is a soccer player turned coach named Yoon Hong-Dae. The director of this film is Lee Byeong-heon.

Military Service

He was only 19 years old when he enrolled for mandatory military service. The actor began serving his country in the year 2008. Later in the year 2010, the actor was discharged after the completion of the mandatory service.
