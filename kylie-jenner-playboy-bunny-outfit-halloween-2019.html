Kylie Jenner Wore a Playboy Bunny Costume for Halloween 2019

January 2024 Â· 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner is most definitely enjoying Halloween 2019.

With a couple more days to go until October 31, Jenner is proving that every day is Halloween with an array of incredible outfits. After a pitch-perfect Madonna impression, Jenner channeled her inner Playboy Bunny in two different outfits.

Firstly, the makeup mogul wore a baby-pink look, complete with a stunning blonde wig, as she posed with two of her friends.

Then, Jenner opted for a classic, and even more sultry, all-black Bunny.

She even wore light blue contact lenses for the transformation.

Meanwhile, Jenner's daughter with Travis Scott, Stormi Webster, wore an incredibly accurate re-creation of her mom's getup for this year's Met Gala. The lilac dress was accentuated by Stormi's light purple-and-gray wig, truly turning her into her Mom's mini-me. She looked endlessly cute.

Jenner's Playboy party looks come after the Kylie Skin creator re-created Britney Spears and Madonna's iconic performance at the 2003 MTV VMAs, when the singers kissed onstage. While Jenner dressed up as Madonna, her BFF, Anastasia "Stassie" Karanikolaou, wore a Spears costume. And, yes, they even reenacted the famous kiss.

Jenner perfected her Madonna look in an all-black outfit, which she paired with a blonde wig and a dark red lip.
