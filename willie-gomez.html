Willie Gomez is a singer and a talented dancer from Miami. He recently auditioned for “ The Voice “ and sang the song  “La Bachata” for his blind performance. He sang so well that all the judges were totally mesmerized by his voice.

Early Life

Willie was born on 29th Dec 1989, in Dominican Republic and raised in the city of Miami. His sister’s name is Diana. Willie first had an intense affection and enthusiasm for music when he was a young child. He participated in singing, dancing, and acting classes to start developing his career into what it is now.

At a young age, he was inspired by Pop artists and decided to become a singer one day at 15 years old began dance and singing training at MDA Studios right away. At age 17, he had already shared the stage with Latin performers like Thalia, Paulina Rubio, India, and David Bisbal.

Willie Gomez Career  

Willie relocated to Los Angeles in the hopes of being successful in the pop music business. He and his abilities were rapidly recognized, and success followed in a flurry. He was a dancer when his professional career started. Willie performed with many superstars including Britney Spears, Christina Aguilera, Katy Perry, Kesha, Kylie Minogue, Chayanne, Little Mix, and Mary J. Blige.
