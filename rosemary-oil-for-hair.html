What are the benefits of rosemary oil?

  • It can help thicken hair. Research shows that rosemary oil may be as beneficial as minoxidil—the active ingredient in Rogaine—for hair growth (but more on that in a minute).
  • It’s antimicrobial. According to King, rosemary oil has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that can soothe the scalp.
  • It helps minimize dandruff. Those antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties “help to keep dandruff and scalp acne at bay,” King says.
  • It helps prevent breakage. Some hair thinning is due to breakage. According to Pucciarello, rosemary oil helps prevent thinning from aging or hormonal conditions.
  • It’s safe for all hair types. Most everyone can use rosemary oil, although “if you have any allergic reactions, stop using the product and consult your dermatologist,” Pucciarello adds.

How should rosemary oil be applied?

Rosemary oil can be applied daily or after every wash, either directly or through a number of care products. “If you’re hoping to address hair growth, apply directly onto the scalp and massage in for a couple minutes,” Colombini advises. “If you’re hoping to address hair breakage or dryness, apply to the hair to act as a conductor to help transfer sebum—hair’s natural oils— onto the hair shaft, which acts as a protector.”
