Kendall Jenner's Old Hollywood glamour guide to street style

August 2024 · 2 minute read

You can always rely on Kendall Jenner for some celebrity style inspiration (who could forget her viral tights and briefs ensemble?) and this week the model stepped out in another noteworthy look.

Jenner put an Old Hollywood spin on her street style, stepping out in Paris looking like a modern-day Audrey Hepburn. Her head-to-toe look by The Row consisted of a black dress and matching headscarf; a perfectly timeless look that serves as yet another reminder that the Little Black Dress is one of the wisest investments you can make when wanting to take a capsule classic approach to your wardrobe.

BACKGRID

Here's why we love it and how to style the look for yourself:

The Look

Jenner looked effortless in an elegant black dress by The Row – with a chic ankle-skimming length and scooped neckline – from the brand's spring/summer 2023 collection. She styled it with a sheer headscarf, black sunglasses and pointed black pumps. While her exact look isn't available to shop just yet, you can recreate it with similar pieces below.

Celine cat-eye sunglasses

Why it works

The model and reality TV star's street-style moment embodies Old Hollywood glamour – echoing Audrey Hepburn's fashion in Breakfast at Tiffany's – thanks to the simple-but-effective addition of the headscarf and vintage-feel sunglasses. She's clearly having fun with fashion and promoting the idea of dressing up anywhere, any time, no matter the occasion.

Why it's a forever classic

The Little Black Dress is the ultimate capsule classic: it never dates, goes with everything in your wardrobe and looks effortlessly elegant – simply throw it on and off you go – making it a smart, responsible piece to invest in. When it comes to shopping more sustainably, it always pays to consider wearability and quality.
