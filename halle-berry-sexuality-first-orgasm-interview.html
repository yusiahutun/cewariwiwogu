Halle Berry Talks About Her First Orgasm &amp; Exploring Sexuality

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Halle Berry is getting real about sexuality, and the first time she ever had an orgasm.

In an episode of her IGTV series, "Bad & Booshy," this week, Berry spoke with her friend and stylist, Lindsay Flores, about discovering her sexuality at a young age. "I remember my first orgasm," Berry explained (via People). "I did it to myself." The Monster's Ball star went on to reveal that she was 11 years old when she orgasmed for the first time.

Berry went on to explain that she'd been "figuring out my sexuality—like most girls" when she had her first experience. Meanwhile, Flores said that she couldn't remember her own first orgasm, to which Berry retorted, "Shame on you ... I feel bad for you now."

"I feel bad for myself," Flores joked.

It was recently reported that Berry is in a new relationship with singer and producer Van Hunt. A source told People that the couple has been dating for "several months," and that Hunt has already met Berry's children: 12-year-old daughter Nahla Ariela Aubry and 6-year-old son Maceo Robert Martinez.

People's source further revealed, "They are spending a lot of time at Halle's Malibu house ... She really seems to enjoy the quiet time. Van has met her kids, so it seems pretty serious." Berry confirmed the relationship on Instagram by wearing a T-shirt emblazoned with Hunt's name. She captioned the sweet snap, "Now ya know," leading fans to congratulate the actress on her new romance.
