How We Determined the Best Drugstore Foundations for Oily Skin?

While on our hunt for the best drugstore foundations for oily skin, we looked for formulas infused with skin-loving and oil-absorbing ingredients, including salicylic acid, along with high-performing makeup pigments. We also searched for words like “matte” and “shine-free” while looking for oil-free formulas that can make your skin greasy in the long run. To note, oily skin tends to produce excess sebum and the pores easily get clogged, resulting in acne, dark spots, and breakouts. It becomes very hard for oily skin to control the film of sheen that seeps through the makeup and forms on top, melting away everything. Using oil-blotting sheets carries the danger of the makeup getting wiped away while layering with powder results in caking and creasing. We have picked out some of the best oil-free drugstore foundations that do not sit heavy on the skin and deliver an airbrush effect without clogging the pores. They are specifically made for oily and acne-prone skin with non-comedogenic formulas and mattified finishes. Boasting of lasting round-the-clock without transferring or smudging or melting, these foundations have a wide array of shades and are extremely cost-effective.

    How to Choose a Drugstore Foundation for Oily Skin?

    Choosing a drugstore foundation for oily skin can be quite overwhelming with so many products available. The pointers mentioned below might be able to help you in doing so:

    • Formulation: Foundations for oily skin should be oil-free. A formulation that is shine-controlling and gives off a matte finish is ideal.
    • Non-comedogenic: Oily skin tends to suffer from clogged pores due to the production of excess sebum. Always look for non-comedogenic formulas that do not clog pores with dirt or excess oil.
    • Coverage: Foundations with lightweight coverage that allows the skin to breathe is suitable for oily skin. As the facial skin remains covered in makeup, it is essential to use products that let the skin breathe.
    • Customer Reviews: Last but not least, customer reviews can be your unexpected guide while choosing the right foundation for oily skin. The reviews will let you know how well a foundation performs regarding oily skin and its accompanying complications.

    How to Use Drugstore Foundations for Oily Skin?

    When you have oily skin, prepping up your skin before applying foundation is as important as finding the right foundation. Follow the steps mentioned below to lock in your foundation for a flawless makeup finish:

    Cleanser: A clean base is essential for the foundation to set properly. Cleansing will remove all the dirt, oil, and impurities, leaving behind clear, hydrated skin. Select a cleanser for oily skin and with a cotton pad, gently clean your face and neck.

    Moisturizer: Moisturizing provides the required hydration to oily skin. Select an oil-free and non-comedogenic formula with hyaluronic acid as it reduces the appearance of pores, hydrating and plumping up the skin.

    Primer: Our contributor Maya Adivi, a certified makeup artist, shares, “You can use a primer without foundation, even if you have oily skin! Using a primer alone can help blur the look of the pores and provide some oil-control, but without the heaviness or need for blending that can come with foundation.”

    Primer is necessary to control shine and make sure the makeup stays for a longer period. Choose a matte primer and prime your face, including the lips and eyelids.

    Powder: Dust a light layer of loose powder on the T-zone before applying the foundation. It will lock in makeup and prevent oiliness.

    Foundation: Select a foundation with a mattifying and long-wearing formula designed to prevent shine. Avoid foundations that claim to be dewy or luminous. A matte finish powder foundation is best for oily skin. Take a foundation brush or a blending sponge and lightly cover your face and neck with the foundation. Build a light coverage without looking cakey. Finish with a layer of pressed powder when using a liquid foundation for it to set correctly. You are ready to apply the rest of your makeup products.

    FAQ's

    Liquid foundation Vs. Powder foundation – which is good for oily skin?

    Powder foundation is ideal for oily skin as it will give a matte finish. It helps in controlling the shine, adding lightweight coverage.

    Are stick foundations good for oily skin?

    Yes, stick foundations are good for oily skin as once they are applied, they remain set, blending well and offering a smooth texture. They give full coverage, concealing blemishes and absorbing the excess oil.

    Can foundations cause pimples?

    Yes, foundations can cause pimples and breakouts in acne-prone and sensitive skin. Opting for a mattifying foundation that is suitable for your skin type and following a proper skincare routine will prevent acne.

    Can people with oily skin wear foundation?

    Yes, people with oily skin can wear foundation, but a foundation should be selected specifically for oily skin. A matte finish foundation with lightweight coverage is best suited.

    Are water-based foundations good for oily skin?

    Yes, water-based foundations are good for oily skin as they blend easily, getting absorbed into the skin without feeling heavy. They do not clog pores and oxidize less.

    What are the best clean drugstore foundations for oily skin?

    Some of the best clean drugstore foundations for oily skin are Maybelline New York Fit Me Matte + Poreless Liquid Foundation, e.l.f. Flawless Finish Foundation, Maybelline New York Super Stay Liquid Foundation, Liquid Foundation by Revlon, and L'Oreal Paris Infallible Pro-Matte Liquid Foundation. They are oil-free, non-comedogenic, control shine, and do not clog pores.
