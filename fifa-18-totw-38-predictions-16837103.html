FIFA 18 TOTW 38 Predictions: FUT Team Of The Week coming ahead of big FIFA 19 reveal

February 2024 Â· 3 minute read

Right now FIFA 18 players might be more concerned with assembling their new squads with latest FIFA 18 Team of the Season squads, or possibly, looking to the future with a potential FIFA 19 announcement coming later this week from EA play ahead of E3 2018.

However, for the time being, EA Sports also have the games 38th Team of the Week squad to release on the PS4, Xbox One, PC and Nintendo Switch Ultimate Team mode. For anyone new to FIFA Ultimate team TOTW, here's a quick overview:

The FUT Team of the Week (TOTW) is (usually) a weekly team of 23 in-form player cards and these players will be boosted with a stat upgrade by EA based on how well they have performed over the last week in real-life football fixtures.

However, in the off season it's slightly small, usually consisting of just 18.

The new FIFA 18 Ultimate Team cards are available from packs in-game but will only be available for one week from 6PM (UK time) every Wednesday. They can also be found in the in-game market, but usually at hugely inflated prices.

Now onto business - while we can't say for definite who will be in the 18 man TOTW squad, there are one or two players who have surely cemented their place this week following some fine performances in the last round of fixtures.
