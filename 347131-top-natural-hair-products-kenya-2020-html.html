Top natural hair products in Kenya 2020

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The natural hair enthusiastic community is growing fast in the country. Lots of people are currently ditching relaxers and other related commodities to embrace coils and curls. However, learning good hair practices and choosing the best natural hair products in Kenya is not a walk in the park.

Getting perfect African hair means that you are using some of the best products in the market. Many local brands are currently producing Kenyan natural hair products that are pocket friendly.

List of the best natural hair products in Kenya

Below are 10 products you can use when it comes to styling natural hair:

1. Keyara Organics

Keyara Organics is known for producing some of the top natural hair products in the country. They range from body balm, massage oil, coconut oil, and body butter. The company also manufactures almond oil shower gel and aloe vera. Their shea nut and marula oil will leave your hair shinning like never before.

2. Akusi Organics

One of the notable Akusi Organics products is their natural hair shampoo. It is crafted to give your hair the best shine leaving it in its high maintenance texture. Your curls will feel clean and fresh but not stripped, weighed down, or hydrated after using the product.

Akusi Organics goods are also lightly scented. This is an important aspect, especially if you are mixing multiple styling products.

3. Cinnabar Green

Other than natural hair food, Cinnabar Green is also known for some of the best beauty products in Kenya, such as bathing soaps, body wash, scrub, essential oils and bath salts.

Their hair oils ensure that you don’t weigh down your curls. They control frizzing while adding an exceptionally gorgeous and glistening shine that will not get dull later in the day.

4. Ajani Handmade

Ajani Handmade goods range from natural hair and body creations. They include body scrub, essential oils, and hair butter. Their products smell divine and are the best for soothing itchy scalps, reduction of flakes, and hydration.

5. Harvest of Sunshine

Harvest of Sunshine is a leading producer of natural hair products in Kenya. Their leave-in conditioner, hair butter creme, lock and style, and 3 in 1 conditioner are the best for your locks. Your curls will endure less breakage if you use Harvest of Sunshine hair food.

6. Mosara

Mosara produces shampoos, conditioners, coconut oil, leave-in conditioner, and milk moisturizer. Their black hair products smell heavenly and work well. All naturalists know that keeping curls moisturized is the number 1 decree in the top ten curly hair commandments. Using Mosara is one way you can adhere to this rule.

7. Luguah Naturals

Luguah Naturals has some of the best packages for naturalists. One of the fascinating things about their hair butter is the rich ingredients. Some of the ingredients include strawberry, black castor, vanilla, cinnamon and peppermint.

8. Bu.ke

Bu.ke is a leading producer of the best products for natural black hair. From shampoo bars, bentonite clay to hair growth stimulating oils, their goods are of high quality. If you are thinking of shaving and starting over again, try these products before doing so.

Their shampoo bars are crafted to ensure that your locs are deeply cleaned on the roots. It is the only way your hair can come closer to the feeling of being weightless without picking up that pair of scissors or razors.

9. Mugzie’s Naturals

Mugzie’s Naturals is the best brand for naturalists out there. They have goods ranging from hair butter, peppermint, coconut oil, shea butter and cocoa butter. Their products are free from mineral oil, petroleum, and sulfates. Hence, they can create the softest slip on your precious curls.

Bid goodbye to knots by using Marini natural hair products. With lots of creations such as curling butter, hair oil, leave-in conditioner, daily spritz, and shampoo, quality is always at the forefront at Marini Naturals.
