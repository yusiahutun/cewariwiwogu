Who is the owner of Ciroc?

Ciroc is a brand of eau-de-vie vodka, manufactured by using grapes from the Charente-Maritime region of France. Diageo produces and distributes it worldwide. In the USA, it is Sean “Diddy” Combs under his brand Combs Enterprises that handles the marketing and promotion of the drink.

If you are asking yourself, what is Puff Daddy net worth and how did he get it, the answer could be in Ciroc. In 2007, the rapper entered into an agreement with Diageo for the marketing and promotion of the drink. In that agreement, the two parties share profits 50-50 between themselves.

According to Diddy, Ciroc was only moving about 30,000 cases a year at the time he agreed with Diageo. In 2015, he said that the number had increased to over two million cases a year. The average price for a 750 ml bottle of Ciroc is $28.00 on Walmart.
