Biography

Bernie Casey was born in Wyco, WV on June 8, 1939. Former professional football player and Bowling Green track and field star who transitioned into an acting career with notable performances in Brian’s Song, the Bond film Never Say Never Again, and Boxcar Bertha. He portrayed a spoof of himself in Keenen Ivory Wayans‘s film I’m Gonna Git You Sucka, playing a part as a football player turned actor. He made his acting debut in the sequel to The Magnificent Seven called Guns of the Magnificent Seven. Other earlier roles included a starring role as Mike Harris in Harris and Company and a part opposite Burt Reynolds in Sharky’s Machine.
