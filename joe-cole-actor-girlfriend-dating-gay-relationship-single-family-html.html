English actor Joe Cole rose to fame while portraying the character of John Shelby in the series- Peaky Blinders, released in 2013. The crime series now has a total of five seasons as of 2021.

Looking at Cole’s character enjoying a marital relationship with Esme Shelby, played by the actress Aimee-Ffion Edwards many fans wonder about his off-screen relationship. 

Does Actor Joe Cole Have A Wife?

Although Cole’s character relishes a marital life on-screen, his off-screen love life is still under the wraps. 

He has managed to captivate many hearts with his natural acting skills and wonderful portrayals, but despite that, he has never been seen with any lady who could be assumed as his love interest.

Nevertheless, he once made a heart-touching tweet on the occasion of Valentine's Day way back in 2012.

Since then, he has not made any tweets regarding relationships or having a girlfriend/wife.

His quiet off-screen love life has also sparked several rumors considering him as gay. However, the speculations alone cannot justify his sexuality as he has not openly announced anything regarding his sexual orientation. 

Although Joe remains low-key on revealing his love life, he remains open when it comes to the professional aspect of his life.

Short Bio and Career 

Born on 28 November 1988, Joe was raised in a family of five brothers. He is not the only actor from his family, as his brother Finn Cole is an actor too and has even shared the screen with Joe in Peaky Blinders. 

Joe’s acting career began after he was accepted into the National Youth Theatre. The English actor started his television career in 2010 with the series The Bill, where he portrayed Leo Cooper's character.

In the same year, he portrayed the character of Shaun Jackson in Holby City, Lee in Stanley Park, and Michael in a short film Assessment.

Further, Cole also appeared in series like Come Fly with Me, Injustice, Playhouse Presents, Peaky Blinders, and many more.

As of 2021, Cole portrayed the character of Iver Iversen in the movie Against the Ice, and he will be playing the role of Harry Palmer in the upcoming series The Ipcress File.
