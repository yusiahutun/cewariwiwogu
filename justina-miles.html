Justina Miles is a wide-known black deaf ASL performer, and she is also a famous TikTok star. In February 2023, she gained popularity, when she performed at the Super Bowl pre-game and halftime show. During the halftime show, she performed the hit songs Rihanna included B*tch Better Have My Money, Work, Diamonds, and Umbrella. According to analysis, her ASL dance video has been viewed more than 1.5 million times on the internet.

Early Life

Justina Miles was born on 12 September 2002, which makes her 20 years old as of 2023. She was raised in Philadelphia, and dreams to achieve a successful career. Her father’s details are unknown and her mother is also deaf. Furthermore, her other family is mixed with hearing impairment issues. Talking about Justina’s education, she completed her primary education at the Model Secondary School for the Deaf in Washington DC, United States. She was a Valedictorian student and received the honors of the highest-ranked student. Currently, she is a student at HBCU Bowie State University where she is studying nursing.
