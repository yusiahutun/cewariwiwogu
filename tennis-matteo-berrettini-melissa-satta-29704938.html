Tennis star slams claims ex-footballer WAG famed for love of bonking is a distraction

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Tennis star Matteo Berrettini has hit back at claims WAG Melissa Satta is the reason behind his downturn in form.

Berrettini reached a career-high world ranking of No. 6 last year but has slipped down to No. 22 due to a string of disappointing results after returning from a hand surgery which saw him miss the entirety of the clay court season in 2022.

It's coincided with his new relationship with Satta, who was previously married to footballer Kevin-Prince Boateng and memorably attributed his regular injuries to their "seven to 10 times a week" bonking sessions, with reports in their native Italy putting blame on her.
