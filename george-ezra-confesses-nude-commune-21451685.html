George Ezra confesses 'nude commune' was inspiration for next album

May 2024 · 2 minute read

The Shotgun star famously travels to unusual places to find inspiration for his music.

He spilled: “We would wake up and you could paddle through the water in the morning and walk down the beach down one end, and it was quiet...so we said, ‘Let’s walk in that direction.’

“And what we found was one of the only nudist beaches in Essex but I think it’s known for being quite promiscuous.
