The 5 Best Clarifying Shampoos for Natural Hair

May 2024 · 2 minute read

Well folks, here we are again. Another week has come to a close, which means wash day is coming up.

And it's hot as hell, so you've been sweating. Outside is open (for now), so you've been putting hella products all up and throughout your hair before you head out the door. Plus, you know, you've been at the park, so surely the wind has blown some outdoor debris into your 'fro.

Thankfully, no matter how dirty your curls and coils may be, clarifying shampoos can get rid of it all. Consider these formulas a fresh start that you can use about once or twice a month (depending on your hair type) to get your strands in tip-top shape.

And since we're such a fan of them, we decided to share our five all-time favorite clarifying shampoos for natural hair with you.

Mizani Moisture Fusion Gentle Clarifying Shampoo

If your hair tends to be on the drier side, but you still need a good cleanse, this is the shampoo for you. Formulated with charcoal and coconut oil, this product will remove all buildup without leaving your hair feeling like something a horse should eat.

Kinky-Curly Come Clean Shampoo

This formula not only helps to remove buildup and hard water minerals from the hair and scalp, but it's also paraben, sulfate, and cruelty-free.

Moroccanoil Clarifying Shampoo

This clarifying shampoo may strip away excess oil and dirt, but it won't make your color budge. Plus, the signature Moroccanoil scent is divine.

As I Am Curl Clarity Shampoo

Coconut, amla, and tangerine come together to create this creamy cleanser that will rid your hair of everything you don't want — and leave in everything you need: all those good, natural oils.

Mixed Chicks Shampoo

Mixed Chicks' vegan formula is perfect for anyone with delicate, fine curls. And while the rich lather will give you a deep cleanse, the shampoo's blend of avocado and borage seed oils will leave your hair super shiny.
