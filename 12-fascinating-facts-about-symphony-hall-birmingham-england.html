Architectural Brilliance

Symphony Hall is an architectural marvel, designed by Percy Thomas Partnership. The venue’s unique crescent-shaped exterior, made from Portland stone, beautifully blends modern and classical elements.

Acoustic Excellence

Symphony Hall is acclaimed for its exceptional acoustics, considered among the finest in the world. The hall’s design incorporates unique features and materials to ensure optimal sound quality, creating a mesmerizing auditory experience for both performers and audience members.

Home of The City of Birmingham Symphony Orchestra

Symphony Hall is the official home of The City of Birmingham Symphony Orchestra (CBSO), one of the leading orchestras in the UK. The venue’s outstanding acoustics provide the perfect setting for the orchestra to showcase their talent and deliver unforgettable performances.

Musical Variety

The hall boasts a diverse program, hosting a wide range of musical genres and artists. From classical symphonies and operas to contemporary pop concerts, Symphony Hall offers something for every musical taste.

International Recognition

Known for its exceptional sound quality, Symphony Hall has gained international recognition as one of the finest concert halls in the world. It has received numerous accolades and has been praised by musicians and critics alike.

Capacity and Space

The hall can accommodate over 2,200 people, making it one of the largest concert venues in the UK. With its spacious stage and surrounding seating, Symphony Hall creates a grand atmosphere for both intimate performances and large-scale productions.

Exceptional Sightlines

Every seat in Symphony Hall offers excellent sightlines, ensuring that no audience member misses out on the visual spectacle of a performance. The design of the hall allows for clear views of the stage from all angles.

Central Location

Symphony Hall is situated in the heart of Birmingham, making it easily accessible for both local residents and visitors. Its prime location contributes to its popularity as a cultural hub of the city.

Educational Outreach Programs

Symphony Hall is committed to promoting musical education and engagement. The venue offers various outreach programs, providing opportunities for young musicians and fostering a love for the arts within the community.

Renowned Guest Performers

Over the years, Symphony Hall has hosted renowned guest performers from around the globe. Legendary musicians and orchestras have graced the stage, adding to the venue’s illustrious history.

Symphony Hall Organ

The hall boasts a magnificent organ, known as the Symphony Hall Organ. With its impressive 6,000 pipes and exquisite sound, it adds richness and depth to performances, enhancing the overall musical experience.

Magnificent Chandelier

A focal point of Symphony Hall’s interior is the breathtaking chandelier suspended above the auditorium. Comprised of thousands of crystals, it adds a touch of elegance and grandeur to the space, creating a truly enchanting ambiance.
