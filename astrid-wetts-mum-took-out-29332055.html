Speaking on the LowIQ podcast, Astrid said: "My older sister left and my mum went crazy, I don't really know what happened.

"I was in college and my mum had found out I was having sex with my boyfriend. So at this point I was 16 going on 17. She found out and she went mental.

"She said 'you're never seeing him again, you're never leaving this house again. I can't believe you're having sex with men'. At this point she'd already, basically, ruined my life.

It got even worse for Astrid though, who was pulled out of college and work by her mum and, as the she was "locked in my room".

Astrid recalled this moment as the last time she would see her mum, as her actions prompted her to leave.

The OnlyFans star continued: "She literally locked me in my room. I remember the only thing I had in my bedroom was a little watch to tell the time because she took every electronic item.

"I literally picked up my little watch, my passport. Didn't have a phone, didn't have a bank account, ran out the house into the pouring rain. Just ran away. That was the last time I saw her."
