Kylie Jenner Wears a Leather Corset Skirt with a Backstreet Boys T-Shirt

April 2024 · 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner continues her skin-tight outfit streak with another figure-fitting look on Instagram. In her latest OOTD pic, the 21-year-old beauty mogul wears a snug black leather corset skirt with—wait for it—a Backstreet Boys band tee.

Her skirt, by Epuzer, is made of panels of natural Italian leather held together by tiny, intertwining stripes. For a star who's worn everything from latex looks to sheer dresses, this piece fits right in with Kylie's seductive wardrobe.

Shop Kylie's Skirt

Jenner wasn't even alive yet when then the Backstreet Boys released their debut album in 1996 (she was born in '97), but don't worry, she was around to appreciate all of the hits that followed. Her tee, by Rhude, features the '90s pop quintet—with baby faces, frosted tips, and all—covered in Swarovski crystal accents. To top off the nostalgic vibe, she accessorized with a mini shoulder bag.

This wasn't the first time a Jenner crossed paths with the iconic boy band. In 2015, Kylie's sister, Kendall, walked for the H&M x Balmain show in New York, where the Backstreet Boys reunited for a surprise performance. (Kylie sat front row.)

Just days ago, Kylie appeared to pay a fashionable homage to another '90s pop icon, Britney Spears. Jenner posed in a see-through beaded look by Yousef Aljasmi reminiscent of the singer's NSFW bodysuit in "Toxic."

Jenner also showed off her newly bleached lob, which she debuted on social media over the weekend. She made the reveal with a casual selfie she shared from a casual, behind-the-scenes moment on a photoshoot.
