Emma Bridgewater's New Coronation Collection is 30% off

January 2024 · 3 minute read

Just in time for the big day! British pottery brand Emma Bridgewater is running a 30% off sale on its special King Charles III Coronation range from today until May 9th.

In fact, all of the brand’s Royal collections are discounted, including on its selection of Queen Elizabeth II commemorative pieces.

The sale features plenty of royal-themed designs on some of the brand’s best-loved pieces, including some lovely half-pint mugs, a selection of crockery, tea towels, storage tins, serving trays and napkins, among other treasures.

Charles is set to take the throne on Saturday 6th May 2023, alongside Camilla, Queen Consort, who will be crowned at the same time. The historic event will be the first Coronation in nearly 70 years following our late Queen Elizabeth II’s record-breaking reign.

There are three new brand new designs created in Charles' honour, comprising everything you'd expect to find from Emma Bridgewater, with prices starting from £10. Of course, you'll find some lovely new half-pint mugs, along with a selection of crockery, tea towels, storage tins, serving trays and napkins.

In the '3 Cheers for King Charles' print, there are 10 beautiful pieces, including mugs, tea towels, plates, tea caddies and serving trays. This design features a vibrant hand-painted crown and lion motif and the date of the Coronation in a traditional red, gold and purple colour scheme. One to add some royal glamour to your kitchen – or some extra smartness when serving your bakes at your local street party!
