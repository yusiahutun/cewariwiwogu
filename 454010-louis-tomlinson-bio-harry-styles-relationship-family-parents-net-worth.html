Early life

Louis Tomlinson was born on December 24, 1991, in Doncaster, South Yorkshire, England. His parents were Troy Austin and Johannah Poulston, and they gave him the name Louis Troy Austin. Unfortunately, Troy and Johannah separated a few days after his birth.

After Louis Tomlinson’s parents divorced, his mother raised him independently, but later met and married Mark Tomlinson. As a result, he adopted his step-father’s last name, changing his official name to Louis William Tomlinson.

Tomlinson has seven half-siblings from his parents' other relationships after splitting up. On his father's side, there is a half-sister, Georgia Austin. After his mother remarried, she had five daughters, Charlotte, Félicite, Daisy, and Phoebe Tomlinson. In addition, Tomlinson has two other siblings, Doris and Earnest Deakin, from his mother’s third marriage.

Tomlinson grew up in Doncaster, where he attended the Hayfield School. After failing his A-Levels, he transferred to Hall Cross School (Hall Cross Academy), completing his education. He was exposed to the showbiz industry from a young age.

Alongside two of his sisters, he appeared as an extra in Fat Friends, a British drama series. Following this, he attended an acting school at Barnsley to improve his skills. He also appeared in several musicals while attending high school. Aside from acting, he was interested in music and was even in a band but was kicked out after another lead singer joined them.

Early career beginnings

After appearing in Fat Friends, Tomlinson had minor acting roles in films and TV shows such as Waterloo Road (BBC) and If I Had You (ITV film). After finishing high school, he also got several jobs, including working as a waiter. In 2009, he applied for the X-Factor after turning 18 years. Unfortunately, he did not progress past the auditions.

X-Factor and One Direction

In 2010, Tomlinson returned to the X-Factor and auditioned before judges Nicole Scherzinger, Louis Walsh, and Simon Cowell. All three judges gave him a yes, progressing him to the Bootcamp. However, he failed to move through to the 'Boys' category.

Tomlinson was advised to be in a group and was put together with contestants Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, and Zayne Malik. The group became known as One Direction, following Harry Styles' suggestion. They competed in the Groups category, finishing third in the finals.

After the X Factor, Simon Cowell signed One Direction through his record label, Syco Records. In September 2011, they released their debut single, What Makes You Beautiful, a worldwide hit. Soon after, they released their debut album, Up All Night, also a commercial success.

While part of One Direction, Tomlinson released five albums with the group. However, soon after the release of the fifth album, the group went on a hiatus to "pursue solo projects."

Judge and mentor

After One Direction, Tomlinson pursued several other projects. This time, he returned to X factor as a judge and a mentor for the contestants. He also worked on his solo music career, releasing his first song, Just Hold On, as a collabo with DJ Steve Aoki.

Football career

Aside from acting and music, Tomlinson is also a football fan. He used to run a pub team, Three Horseshoes and even played in a charity game to raise money. Louis Tomlinson and Eleanor Calder started dating in 2011 but split up in 2015. However, they got back together in 2017 and have been in a long-term relationship.

Aside from Calder, Tomlinson has been linked romantically with several other people. He dated actress Danielle Campbell briefly around 2015 and 2016.
