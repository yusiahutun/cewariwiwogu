KUCCPS contacts: phone numbers, email and other details

February 2024 · 3 minute read

The Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS) is a corporate agency that is mandated to coordinate placement of students under the government sponsorship to universities and colleges. The placement is done through the career guidance and later placement to institutions of higher learning for the award of certificates, diplomas and degrees in the various fields of expertise. As a student, these KUCCPS contacts will help you to make applications for various courses and later submit through the student portal.

The core mandate of KUCCPS is to promote equity in the access to university, college and tertiary education. KUCCPS attends to students directly through the KUCCPS customer care desk. We shall look at the official KUCCPS contacts and find the easiest way to access them for enquiries and contacts.

KUCCPS contacts

There are numerous reasons why anyone would like to know the KUCCPS Kenya contacts. The contacts are crucial to students who are awaiting placement in the various institutions of higher learning. Other than knowing where the KUCCPS office is located, it is paramount that anyone seeking the KUCCPS services accesses the working KUCCPS phone numbers.

Services provided by KUCCPS

As a body that is mandated with the placement role for all the students, KUCCPS provides numerous services that are geared towards enabling the students to both access and realize their career dreams through training in institutions of higher learning.

It provides career guidance and counselling through the KUCCPS email and KUCCPS contacts before the actual placement. Career advisory is attained through the career advisory messaging platform in conjunction with the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA).

KUCCPS provides inter-institution transfer for applicants who wish to change their current institutions of higher learning. Through the KUCCPS help contacts, a student can make inquiries on the process and concerns about inter-varsity transfers.

KUCCPS also offers other relevant guidance services. This includes programs that are available to the student in each university, the subject clusters for every course, weighed cluster points as well as all other forms of downloads. Students with enquiries can contact the body through the working KUCCPS contacts outlined in this article.

Unlike Joint Admissions Board (JAB) that only facilitated admission of students to public universities, KUCCPS has taken much responsibility by providing and placing government sponsored students to both private and public universities and colleges across the country.
