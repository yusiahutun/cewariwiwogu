Early Life and Education

Born on January 18, 1988, in Atlanta, Georgia, Kristen was raised by parents Roger Ledlow and Rita Ledlow. She also has a sibling, Lauren Ledlow. She comes from a white ethnic background and is of American nationality.

Kristen is a graduate of Christian Community School. For her further studies, she attended North Florida Christian School. In addition, she majored in broadcasting and communications and minored in business at Southeastern University in Lakeland.
