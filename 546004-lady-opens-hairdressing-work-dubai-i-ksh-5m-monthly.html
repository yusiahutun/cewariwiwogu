Lady Opens Up About Her Hairdressing Work In Dubai: &quot;I Make KSh 5m Monthly&quot;

May 2024 · 4 minute read
  • A woman who owns a beauty salon in Dubai has disclosed how much money she makes from her business in a month
  • Famously known as Ages, the young woman, in a video shared on Facebook, says she makes KSh KSh 5 million per month from her hairdressing business
  • Netizens thronged the comment section of the video with varied reactions

A woman who relocated to Dubai, UAE, over a year ago to seek greener pastures has opened up about her earnings in the Arabian city.

Madam Joyce, popularly known as Ages, says she makes up to KSh 5 million per month working as a hairdresser.

She added that she pays 12,000AED, equivalent to KSh 438,441, every three months as rent for the shop where she operates her salon.

Ages says her beauty salon employs a lot of people in Dubai and some from Africa, adding that some of the workers she sponsored from home to come and work for her have jilted her for other companies.

Why Joyce relocated to Dubai

Speaking to Sammy Kay Media, Ages said she decided to permanently relocate to Dubai and start a life there on her third visit because she realised she could make more money in the Middle Eastern country.

She compared the exchange rate of the dirham currency to the cedis.

“The money here is heavier than the cedis, and on my third visit, I decided to stay permanently and find something to do. I'm a professional hairdress, but I was operating a boutique in Ghana and so when I moved here, I realised there were a lot of boutiques here and so I decided to open a salon after doing some menial jobs here to raise money," she said.

Although she is making some good money in Dubai, Ages said working in the country was challenging because they do not get enough time to rest.

Kisii male hairdresser shares challenges

In other news, Joel Mariba, a male hairdresser in Kisii town, was inspired to learn the skills of friends thriving in the field.

He joined Beauty Point School in Nairobi in 2016 and can now do artificial locs, braids, regular braids, gel extensions, etc.

Among the challenges faced by the talented hairdresser are women who hit on him, seeking to enjoy some of his money. When he refuses their advances, they send others on the mission.
