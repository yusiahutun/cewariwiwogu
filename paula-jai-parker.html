Biography

Paula Jai Parker was born in Cleveland, OH on August 19, 1969. Actress and comedian known for her roles in Friday and Hustle and Flow. She also provided the voice of Trudy Proud in animated series The Proud Family. In 2016 she portrayed Margarita Jean-Baptiste in the drama series Recovery Road. She briefly appeared on the Wayans Brothers show with Shawn Wayans. She graduated from Howard University and shortly after moved to New York in order to perform at comedy clubs.
