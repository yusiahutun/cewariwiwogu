TikTok Comic Goes Viral for Hilarious, Near Accurate Mimic of William Ruto: &quot;Sijui Kama Tunaelewana&quot;

June 2024 · 3 minute read
  • A TikToker @MC Kibe the heavy thinker, shared a clip hilariously mimicking President William Ruto
  • In the viral video, Kibe did an impression of President William Ruto addressing the flooding crisis in the country
  • The comedian's impressive mimicry of the head of state's mannerisms and speech wowed several netizens

A Kenyan man has delighted netizens after doing a spot-on impression of President William Ruto.

How did the man mimic President William Ruto?

In a video, the man who goes by @MC Kibe the heavy thinker on TikTok, made a few remarks regarding the flooding crisis in the country, which has claimed several lives and rendered others homeless.

Addressing an imaginary reporter, the impersonator responded to questions about the floods and the government strategy to tackle the emergency.

Kibe masterfully mimicked the president's distinct mannerisms and facial expressions in the clip.

He used several hand gestures throughout his performance, characteristic of Ruto.

He even included a few phrases the head of state tends to use in his speech.

Moreover, he was dressed in a cap and a coat that looked just like the president's signature Kaunda suit.

"Kwanza kabisa sisi kama serikali...mimi nataka nichukue hii nafasi kuwapa pole ile familiar zote ambazo wamepata maafa kwa sababu ya mafuriko. Lakini, ile mambo tuko nayo na ile mambo tumepanga kama serikali...kwa mfano...wale watu wa kukata maji sasa wako na maji ya kukata, wale watu wa kupiga story chini ya maji sasa wako na maji ya kupiga story chii yake...tunaelewana..na vile vile...sijui kama unanielewa wewe mtu ya habari," the comedian said in part.

He then unveiled his 'ingenious' plans to mitigate the flooding problem.

Man imitates Raila Odinga

In another story, a man caused excitement online with his impressive imitation of Azimio la Umoja leader Raila Odinga.

The video, which was widely shared, depicted the TikToker's uncanny ability to mimic Raila's distinctive walking and speaking style.

In the video, McRAO was walking with a limp that closely resembled Raila's distinctive gait.

He even took the extra step of using a handkerchief to wipe his eyes, a characteristic gesture frequently associated with the famous politician.
