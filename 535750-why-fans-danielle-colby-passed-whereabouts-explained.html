Why fans think Danielle Colby passed away: Her whereabouts explained

February 2024 · 4 minute read

Fans were curious about Danielle Colby's whereabouts for the period when she suddenly stopped appearing in the popular reality TV show American Pickers. This triggered speculation among fans, the leading rumour being Danielle Colby passed away. So, what happened to Danielle Colby?

Danielle Colby is an American reality TV personality famous for starring in American Pickers. She plays the shop manager role in the series and has been part of it since 2009. Danielle's last appearance on the show was in season 23, episode 14, "Pool Hall Picking," which worried her fans.

Why did Danielle Colby leave American Pickers?

The rumour that Colby passed away American Pickers' was untrue. So why did she leave American Pickers, and what is Danielle Colby's illness? In late 2022, Danielle Colby underwent a hysterectomy after suffering from incredibly painful uterine fibroids. She admitted to losing her desire to work after the tiring surgery.

Did Danielle leave American Pickers because of Frank’s issue?

There was a rumour that Danielle quit American Pickers because of Frank’s issue and more. However, all these allegations against Danielle from American Pickers were untrue – she took a break from the show because of health issues.

For an extended period, American Pickers enjoyed a drama-free run until March 2020, when Frank abruptly ceased appearing on the show. Frank disclosed in an interview with The US Sun that he had been grappling with a back injury. Surprisingly, after undergoing treatment, his co-star and boss, Mike Wolfe, did not try to reach out and inquire about his well-being.

Additionally, he claimed that despite recovering, the network remained evasive in providing a clear response regarding his potential return to the reality show. Subsequently, Frank was replaced on American Pickers by Mike's brother, Robbie, a decision that elicited mixed emotions from various quarters, including, the show's fanbase and Danielle Colby.
