Ariana DeBose Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

June 2024 · 4 minute read

Ariana DeBose is an American actress, dancer, and singer who has worked in various theatres, films, and TV series. She was recognized first on a reality show called So You Think You Can Dance and apart from this, did appear in many films and TV Shows like The Prom, West Side Story, Hamilton, Seaside, and many more. She is also a trained dancer. Ariana DeBose was born on 25th January 1991 in North Carolina, United States of America. Her father’s name is not shared but he is Afro- Puerto Rican and her mother, Gina DeBose is a secondary school teacher. She went to CC &Co. Dance Complex in Raleigh, North Carolina to be a trained dancer. She is a high school graduate and while attending Western Carolina University for three months she pursued her acting career.

Ariana DeBose Career

Her career began when she participated in a reality TV series named So You Think You Can Dance and made it under top 20. Later she worked in a soap opera play named One Life to Live where she played the role of Inez. In 2011 and 2012 she performed in a musical play called Bring it On at Alliance Theatre, National Tour, and St. James Theatre where her role was of Nautica. The next year she performed at Lunt-Fontanne Theatre on Motown: The Musical. In 2014 she was seen in Pippin at Music Box Theatre. In 2015 she was a part of a popular play called Hamilton: An American Musical at The Public Theatre and Richard Rodgers Theatre.

She made her acting debut in TV series named Blue Bloods and Hamilton’s America in 2016. Later she also played the role of Jane in a play called A Bronx Tale at Longacre Theatre. In 2017 and 2018 she was in a play named Summer: The Donna Summer Musical where she was Disco Donna at La Jolla Playhouse and Lunt-Fontanne Theatre. She also appeared in a film called Seaside in 2018. In 2020 she appeared in a Netflix original musical comedy film called The Prom where she plays the role of Alyssa Greene. The next she will appear in West Side Story as Anita, the film is currently under production. She is also working for an upcoming musical comedy TV series called Schmigadoon where she is the main lead playing the role of Emma Tate.

Awards and Nominations

In 2018 she has been nominated for her work in Summer: The Donna Summer Musical at Drama League Awards for Distinguished Performance and at Tony Awards for Best Performance by a Featured Actress in a Musical.
