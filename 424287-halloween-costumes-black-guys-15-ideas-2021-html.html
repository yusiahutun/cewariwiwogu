Halloween costumes for black guys: 15 best ideas in 2021

September 2024 · 8 minute read

Black guys love Halloween simply because they get to dress up as the characters of their imagination. There is no dearth of characters or themes that men can choose from for the perfect costume. The best part is that the Halloween costumes for black guys can be worn to a work party or one that is organized by friends and family.

Halloween festival is well known in the United States, and it is the most popular celebration for adults and children. A Halloween party is a time for happiness, enjoyment, creativity and excitement. Nothing spells Halloween like a peculiar costume to leave others in awe of your chosen outfit.

List of Halloween costumes for black guys

With Halloween fast approaching, you must be scrambling to find ideas for your Halloween costumes. Most men prefer to become a zombie, vampire, ghost or pirate on this occasion. If you are a black guy, you can check out these 15 best ideas to pull off in this year's Halloween celebration.

1. Michael B.Jordan as Killmonger Halloween costume

Killmonger is the cousin of King T'Challa of Wakanda in the famous Black Panther movie. He is a homicidal killer out to seek revenge for the death of his father. His costume is complex from his look as a king and his look as a mercenary.

Both looks are cool Halloween costumes for black guys. You will need an oversized wool cardigan, short dreads with a fade, gold Wakanda necklace, dark slim fitted cargo pants, and a large beaded black necklace for this costume.

2. Eddie Murphy as Prince Akeem costume

This costume draws its inspiration from the movie Coming to America. The story tells of an African prince who travels to America in search of his true love while concealing his prince's identity. He finds himself in Queens, New York. You see him wearing a suit in the movie, glazed with a golden necklace and animal fur.

Jay Z rocked in this outfit during the 2015 Halloween celebration. Indeed, it's an ideal black man Halloween costume. The costume includes a white shirt, animal fur, a king's crown, a fake moustache, a black tuxedo, black pants, and an oversize gold chain.

3. Jamie Foxx in the movie Django costume

In the movie Django, Jamie Foxx plays the role of a freed slave on a quest to save his wife. This is one of the best African-American character costumes. It also sends a message on the struggle that black people went through at the hands of White Supremacists.

Items needed for Django costume include forest green corduroy jacket, black button shirt, brown scarf, tan corduroy pants, belted leather gun holster, a fake or toy gun, cowboy hat, and leather glove.

4. Bill Nunn as a Radio Raheem costume

In the film Do The Right Thing, Bill was cast to play the role of a hip hop fanatic who teams up with Buggin Out to persuade Sai (the pizza owner) to include African American figures on his business wall. The outfit he wears in the film should be on your list of black man Halloween costumes.

5. Giancarlo Esposito as Buggin Out costume

The costume draws its inspiration from Do The Right Thing. Buggin Out is unhappy with his local pizza joint and boycotts the place until pictures of black figures are placed on the wall. If you are a loud and outspoken black guy, this would be a plus on your side.

You can bring the old school look back to the grove if you wear this outfit this year. The Buggin Out costume items include white socks, a yellow striped t-shirt, kente shorts, air Jordan 4, round reading glasses, and African medallion necklaces.

6. Wesley Snipes as Blade outfit

Blade is a half-mortal, half-vampire character on a vengeful mission to find his mother's killers and exterminate vampires in the movie Blade. The character has one of the most impressive Halloween costume ideas for an African-American male.

Items needed for Blade Halloween costume include a bulletproof vest, black leather pants, a black long leather coat, black leather gloves, a deep fade haircut, and dark sunglasses.

7. Denzel Washington as Alonzo Harris outfit

Denzel plays the role of Alonzo, a stern cop who is in conflict with a Russian mafia after he locked horns with one of the gang members in the film Training Day. To repay the debt, he has no option to work illegally. He is a hardcore and manipulative individual, and his outfit depicts that.

For this costume, you will need a black button up long sleeve shirt, two cross pendant necklaces, black cargo pants, and a black leather jacket.

8. Chris Tucker as Smokey

The costume is drawn from the drama Friday. The movie is a tale of two friends Smokey and Craig. Craig loses his job, and Smokey convinces him to start smoking weed. This is one of the cool Halloween costumes for black guys.

The list of items for the Smokey costume includes a blue t-shirt, a pair of black chucks, a gold rope chain, a mimic blunt behind the ear, a blue baseball cap, and khaki pants.

9. Prince Purple Rain costume

This is an old school look and one of the best Halloween costume ideas for black people. His entire outfit is a cool purple-themed masterpiece. It is perfect for people who want to stand out but not in a terrifying way. For this costume, you will need a purple long length jacket, a ruffled style dress shirt, black pants, reading glasses, and a hair wig.

10. Spike Lee as Mookie costume

Spike plays the role of a pizza delivery man in Brooklyn, New York. His iconic look in the Brooklyn Dodgers Jersey is one of the best Halloween costume ideas for an African-American male.

This costume includes several items such as red shorts, Brooklyn Dodgers baseball jersey, African medallion leather necklace, white rough and dirty sneakers, black and red socks, and white blue box pizza boxes.

11. Lakeith Stanfield as Cassius Green costume

On the Sorry to Bother You movie poster, you will notice Cassius has a bloodstained white band around his head. The outfit is rough to show the stress he suffered during the fight. Cassius Green's costume items include a purple stripe suit, white long-sleeved shirt, red tie, white headband, high twisted hair, and a white red-stained headband.

12. Winston Duke as M'Baku costume

M'baku is the ferocious Jabari tribe leader and seen during T'Challa's coronation ceremony condemning the-would- be King of failing to protect his father. For the M'Baku costume, you will need a fur hoodie, leather customized sleeveless top, fur wrist guards, and deep high top hair cut.

13. Fat Albert from Fat Albert costume

The TV show is about a group of urban black kids who are taught life hacks in their Philadephia neighbourhood. Fat Albert is known for being wise and civic-minded. His clothing choice perfectly reflects his more intellectual side. The list of items for this costume includes a red sweater, blue jeans, white sneakers, and a black belt to hold the denim pants in place.

14. T’Challa from Black Panther costume

T’Challa wears his Civil War suit at the beginning of the film, during his trip to extract Nakia from her Nigerian spy mission. The suit can be identified by its basket-weave texture and chevron designs on the chest. This costume also includes silver teeth on the collar with a silver ring connecting them.

15. Michael Jackson Thriller costume

The undisputed King of Pop had many unique outfits, but one of his most remembered looks is one he wore in the Thriller music video. The list of items for this costume includes a red leather jacket, red jeans, white socks, black shoes, a black t-shirt, and Jerry curls.
