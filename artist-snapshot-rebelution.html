ARTIST SNAPSHOT: Rebelution

February 2024 · 2 minute read

LOS ANGELES, CA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Santa Barbara based band Rebelution has officially

announced their headlining 2011 “Winter Greens Tour” which will span 44 shows over the course of

January, February, March, and April in 2011. Special guests on the tour will include a hand

selected array of opening acts: hometown friends Iration, reggae legend Junior Reid, upcoming LA

funk cohorts Orgone, and the improvisational grateful dub of Giant Panda Guerilla Dub Squad.

Scheduled to kick off on January 13 with back-to-back shows in San Diego, California at The House

of Blues, the band will also be promoting their EP of remixes entitled “Remix EP,” which will be

officially released on January 11, 2011.

Available wherever digital downloads are sold, “Remix

EP” will consist of six of Rebelution’s most popular tracks that have been remixed by various

artists including Zion-I, Junior Reid, Jacob Hemphill of SOJA, Yeti Beats, Michael G (Easy Star

All-Stars) and Amplive (Zion I).

In addition to the tour, Rebelution is introducing fan

contests where their fans across the nation will have the opportunity to win a chance to partake in

exclusive “Sound Check Sessions,” where a select group of winners are able to watch Rebelution

sound check and rehearse brand new songs before doors. Fans can submit content for video, photo and

art contests on the Rebelution tour site at www.wintergreenstour.com/contests. Categories include “homemade” video description of favorite

Rebelution song and why, best tattoo, best drawing / painting / graphic design, video explaining

their personal environmentally conscious lifestyle, and creating a music video. The band will

decide the winners from each category.

In their time off stage, throughout the tour Rebelution

will also be participating in Q&A sessions through their official Twitter page. Fans can tweet in

their questions for the band to answer.
