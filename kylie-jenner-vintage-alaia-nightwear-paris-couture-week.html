Kylie Jenner Sizzles in Vintage Alaa Nightwear for Dinner in Paris

August 2024 · 2 minute read

As Clueless’s Cher Horowitz once said, “You don’t understand—this is an Alaïa!” But we do understand, and we love it. And so does Kylie Jenner, who wore a sexy vintage ensemble from the “totally important” designer.

The Kylie Cosmetics mogul put on a head-turning display in an inky black crushed velvet bra with thin straps and a matching luxe robe, which she wore off one of her shoulders and largely open to reveal her lush undergarment and create a heart-racing thigh-high slit. The nighttime set hails from the fashion house’s Fall/Winter 1992 collection (and thus could have very well come out of Horowitz’s closet, as Clueless was released in 1995). Jenner made the bedroom look into eveningwear, teaming it with a pair of tall black leather heels by Christian Louboutin. She went sans jewelry for the evening, but glammed up with sultry, bronzy makeup, including a matte brown/nude lip and smoky eye shadow. For a finishing touch, she wore her rich brown tresses in a wet look, parting her hair to the side and tucking it behind one ear.

Jenner took to Instagram on Saturday to share images of herself in the striking outfit, which she wore to dinner at the Hotel Costes in Paris, a couple of nights before. She enjoyed her meal with A-list company, including Florence Pugh, Jennifer Lopez, and big sis Kim Kardashian.

Jenner has been in the romantic city to attend Paris Couture Week. Earlier in the day, she made a grand entrance at the Maison Margiela show in a gorgeous look from the luxury fashion brand. The Khy founder looked majestic in a dreamy, semi-sheer silver sequin dress with a scoop neck and pretty, short sleeves. She paired it elegantly with gauzy gray gloves and chunky white doll-like pumps.
