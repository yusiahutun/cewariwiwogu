The 12 Best Neroli Perfumes of 2024

May 2024 · 4 minute read

Most Iconic Neroli Perfume

TOM FORD Neroli Portofino Eau de Parfum

Most Iconic Neroli Perfume

TOM FORD Neroli Portofino Eau de Parfum

Watteau name-checks Tom Ford's Neroli Portofino as one of the most iconic neroli fragrances. Also featuring notes of Italian bergamot and Sicilian lemon, it's as close to spritzing on the Mediterranean as you can get.

Best Sexy Neroli Perfume

PHLUR Missing Person Eau de Parfum

Best Sexy Neroli Perfume

PHLUR Missing Person Eau de Parfum

Meant to evoke the smell of nude skin, Missing Person is heavy on the white musk. It's far from one-note, though: bergamot, neroli, and nectar add a juiciness, while Australian sandalwood adds warmth.

Best Warm Neroli Perfume

Prada Paradoxe Eau de Parfum

Best Warm Neroli Perfume

Prada Paradoxe Eau de Parfum

Paradoxe has citrusy top notes of Calabrian bergamot, tangerine, and pear, middle notes of neroli bud, orange blossom, and jasmine, and a base of masculine amber, bourbon vanilla, and white musk. Housed in a bottle shaped like Prada's iconic triangular logo, it's also refillable.

Best Floral Neroli Perfume

Le Labo Neroli 36

Best Floral Neroli Perfume

Le Labo Neroli 36

True Le Labo fans know that Santal 33 is only the beginning. With notes of rose, musk, mandarin orange, jasmine, and vanilla, Neroli 36 is a layered floral scent that is a warm complement to the unisex favorite.

Best Natural Neroli Perfume

Abel Golden Neroli

Best Natural Neroli Perfume

Abel Golden Neroli

A warm citrus, Golden Neroli is complemented with grassy matcha, a heart of petitgrain, ylang ylang, and jasmin sambac, and a base of sandalwood, vanilla. What's more, Abel goes against the norm by revealing its full ingredient list—a rarity in the industry, as fragrance is considered a trade secret.

Best Intense Neroli Perfume

Perfumehead Somewhere Extrait de Parfum

Best Intense Neroli Perfume

Perfumehead Somewhere Extrait de Parfum

Some of us like to leave a trail of scent when we leave the room (in a good way). For those people, there's this heady fragrance from Perfumehead. Citrus-forward top notes include neroli (of course), bergamot, red mandarin, and galbanum, opening to a floral heart of cumin, jasmine, orange flower, and orris, and an earthy base of vetiver, patchouli, sandalwood, musk, and oakmoss.

Best Simple Neroli Perfume

L'Artisan Perfumeur Histoire d'Orangers

Best Simple Neroli Perfume

L'Artisan Perfumeur Histoire d'Orangers

Orange blossom, neroli, and musk are a warm combination in this intentionally spare, best-selling scent from the 40-year-old perfume house.

Best Fresh Neroli Perfume

Ayond Taos Blue Eau de Parfum

Best Fresh Neroli Perfume

Ayond Taos Blue Eau de Parfum

Inspired by springtime in Taos, New Mexico, this scent is airy and grounding. It features top notes of neroli, bergamot, bitter orange, and ozonic accord, a heart of blue lilac, freesia, pink pepper, and ylang ylang, and base of cedarwood, vetiver, and elemi.

Best Unisex Neroli Perfume

Leland Francis Stoner Eau De Parfum

Best Unisex Neroli Perfume

Leland Francis Stoner Eau De Parfum

With notes of neroli, palo santo, leather, and woodsmoke, this warm, herbal fragrance is like a time machine to the Summer of Love—in a chic and elevated way, of course.

Best Neroli Perfume for Layering

Dedcool Smiley Face Garden Fragrance

Best Neroli Perfume for Layering

Dedcool Smiley Face Garden Fragrance

All of Dedcool's perfumes are made for layering, so you can spritz Smiley Face Garden on alone or with another scent of your choosing. Leaning into the fresh category, it features a rose top note, wood and neroli heart, and cedarwood, amber, and musk base.

Best Refillable Neroli Perfume

By Far Daydream of a Bingo Queen Fragrance Set

Best Refillable Neroli Perfume

By Far Daydream of a Bingo Queen Fragrance Set

If you're someone who likes to have perfume with you anywhere and everywhere, consider By Far's refillable keychain vessel. The spicy-citrus scent that goes inside it is lovely, too: Top notes of Mandarin and pink pepper essences, a heart of neroli and orange blossom absolute, and a base of amber and cashmere wood.

Best Airy Neroli Perfume

Eau So Purple Noon

Best Airy Neroli Perfume

Eau So Purple Noon

A floral that doesn't feel too sweet or garden-y, this layered scent begins with top notes of fresh ginger, neroli, coconut water, sweet orange and bergamot. It opens to a heart of Moroccan orange flower and geranium before settling into a base of orris, ylang ylang, tuberose, musk, and vanilla.
