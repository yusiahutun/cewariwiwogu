Best Lipsticks in the World

July 2024 · 2 minute read

- Maybe it's the Rihanna effect, or perhaps American ladies just love a classic red lip, but 2013's top-selling lipstick shade in the U.S. was none other than MAC's Ruby Woo. Other countries favoring a crimson pout include France and Italy, which favor Dior's Rouge Dior 999, and Spain, which purchasesd large quantities of Chanel Rouge Allure 104 Passion last year. The Brits prefer a much subtler shade—their top shade is Lancome's L'Absolu Rouge 06 Rose. Yahoo Beauty
