&quot;I'm so excited to be a Clio Peppiatt bride!&quot;

February 2024 · 4 minute read

London-based designer Clio Peppiatt is known for her more-is-more aesthetic, with her signature hand-beaded mini-dresses loved by everyone from Taylor Swift to Lila Moss. So who better to turn to for your second wedding dress, aka the party look? Thankfully she's just released her sophomore bridal collection, which just as you'd expect is full of ivory mini dresses with gorgeous beading detail.

We caught up with the designer to discuss all things bridal, including her own upcoming nuptials...

What made you decide to move into bridal?

Although we launched our first bridal collection in September 2022, I’d actually been working with brides for years through our bespoke service. A lot of their reasons for coming to us were based on the fact there was very little on offer on the bridal market for fashion forward, modern brides looking for after party dresses on their big day. We’ve always specialised in occasionwear and accessories so it felt like a really organic expansion for the brand. At the time, the bridal world still felt quite traditional. I think the idea of what marriage means to us today has modernised and progressed so much culturally and I wanted to reflect those ideas into the very ethos of our bridal collection. I wanted to create pieces that felt contemporary, sexy and fresh and allowed women to still really feel like an elevated version of ‘themselves’ on a their big day. This coincided at a moment where it was becoming normal if not a necessity for many brides to have a party look, so given our expertise and experience of working so directly with bespoke brides it was just such a natural step.

Who is the Clio Peppiatt bride?

The Clio Peppiatt bride isn’t afraid of doing things differently and challenging tradition. Given the craftsmanship of our pieces, I’d also say our bride is well versed with beautiful quality and that matters far more to her then wearing an established big brand. The amount of work that goes into each piece (all of our bridal is hand beaded) holds huge sentimental value to the brides we work with. We often receive messages from previous clients who plan to hold onto their dress for the rest of their lives, planning to eventually pass it on to someone special. We’ve even had brides frame their dresses in the past!

What was the inspiration behind the latest collection?

To me, this collection has a far more romantic feel than our first bridal collection. As always, nature is huge inspiration to me. Standout pieces such as our Olympia styles, take inspiration from the natural, organic textures and shades of shells, decorated with thousands of hand sewn pearls. Florals and crystal sun rays also feature in the embroiders across the collection but in a very modern way.

Is there a specific part of the day that you find brides are coming to you for mostly? Is it the ceremony, the after party, the rehearsal?

So far, we've noticed that brides primarily approach us for their after party or rehearsal looks, and occasionally even for the bachelorette! With this collection, I wanted to explore dresses that could be worn for the main event as well the after party, so we have introduced more gown like silhouettes with our signature embellishment that the brand is renowned for.

As part of our bridal collections, we also release an array of bridal accessories including handbags, veils, and gloves. Our personalisation service has been very popular with bridal parties, allowing clients to add hand-beaded details such as initials to bags as gifts for their Bridesmaids.

What are the bridal trends that you’re seeing at the moment?

After big scale post-pandemic weddings, I’m seeing a general trend towards more intimate affairs with smaller, specially curated events. More than ever, we’re seeing brides looking for something completely special and unique which pairs wonderfully with our bespoke service. Every detail and motif is created especially for the client, creating a piece full of sentimental touches.

You’re getting married this year – will you be wearing any of your dresses?

It would only be right! Actually, as soon as I got engaged I started working on the wardrobe as we’re holding a few events for the wedding over the summer, I’m so excited to experience being a Clio Peppiatt Bride first hand!

Clio Peppiatt's bridal collection is available online from 29 April.
