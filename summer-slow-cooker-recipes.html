    If you're not taking advantage of your slow cooker in the summer months, you're missing out!

    When it's too hot to turn on the oven, avoid the unnecessary heat by cooking low and slow in your slow cooker. It also works wonders alongside summer grilling recipes. A Crock-Pot handles sides like a champ!

    It's easy to make healthy summer Crock-Pot recipes, vegetarian slow cooker recipes, or even kid-friendly meals. (We have all of those and more below!)

    Some of our favorite slow cookers travel well, so you can bring them along with you to the lake house or beach. This summer, include one of these delicious slow cooker recipes in your dinner rotation!
