1. Pink Fiat 131 Abarth

The Fiat 131 Abarth rally car will forever be iconic. It was a Group Four rally car that won its manufacturers' three World Rally Championships (1977, 1978 and 1980). The car's engine is an inline four-cylinder, two litres, four valves per cylinder, and a single Weber carburettor. It produces 140 horsepower, which is adequate for its 2100 lb. weight.

2. BMW E30 M3

What BMW does Tyler the Creator have? Tyler the Creator's BMW E30 M3 is in a custom cream colour. The BMW E30 M3 is a cult classic car because of its aesthetics, handling and engine. The car’s top speed is 146 miles per hour, so it’s no slouch and was extraordinary during its time.

It also has a fantastic balance between fuel efficiency and power. Plus, it handles corners well due to its front-engine design that allows for improved weight distribution.

3. McLaren 675LT

The McLaren 675LT embodies the essence of a supercar: a perfect blend of style, power, precision and exclusivity. Its attention to detail, lightweight and track-focused performance set it apart as the McLaren epitome.

Weighing in at a lithe 2,927 pounds and powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.8-litre V8 making 666 horsepower, the McLaren 675LT moves from zero to 60 mph in a scant 2.9 seconds and has a top speed of 205 mph.

4. Rolls-Royce Cullinan

Tyler, the Creator's Rolls-Royce Cullinan, was briefly shown in the Lemon Head music video. The Cullinan petrol engine is 6750 cc and is available with automatic transmission. Depending upon the variant and fuel type, the Rolls-Royce Cullinan has a 9.5 km/pl mileage.

It is a five-seater 12-cylinder car with a length of 5341mm, a width of 2000 mm and a wheelbase of 3295 mm. Its seating option makes it the only SUV with a glass partition between the rear passengers and luggage compartment. This ultimate refinement ensures uninterrupted comfort and unparalleled sound installation.

5. McLaren MP4-12C HS

The McLaren MP4-12C HS has the performance and pedigree of a true supercar, yet it's wholly different from any Lamborghini or Ferrari. This car enjoyed a short three-year production run; only 3,400 were made.

The HS stands for High Sport. McLaren MP4-12C HS has different wheels and revised aerodynamic features inspired by the 12C GT3, such as revised rear and front-end bumpers, diffusers, larger vents and a modified rear wing.

6. Audi R8

The Audi R8 performance is an engineering powerhouse like the Audi Quattro. It has a civil dual-clutch, making the transmission less vicious and smoother.

In addition, it has exquisite interiors. Some expensive luxury cars go crazy with heavy-duty interiors, but the Audi R8 is simple. Its interiors are chic leather, and a classic gauge cluster enhances the driving experience.

Tyler the Creator has two Lancia Deltas; one is baby blue, and the other is dark blue; they were in part of the WUSYANAME and Lemon Head music videos, respectively.

8. Rolls-Royce Wraith

The Rolls-Royce Wraith is the oldest car in Tyler, the Creator's garage. There's little information about how and when he purchased the car. But it was featured alongside his other cars in the Lemon Head music video.

However, it's worth noting that the 1938 Roll-Royce Wraith remains one of the rarest of all Rolls-Royces because of its short production life. Only about 491 customers had the joy of taking Wraith's delivery before war broke out, and Roll-Royce production focused entirely on aircraft engines.
