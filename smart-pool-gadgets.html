7 Smart Pool Gadgets That Will Make Summer Even Better

June 2024 · 6 minute read

Summer is the time to relax by the pool and enjoy the warm weather. But why settle for just lounging around when you can make your pool experience even better with these smart gadgets?

Let's dive into our list and find the perfect gadget to take your pool game to the next level.

1. Robot Pool Cleaner

The days of manually cleaning your pool are over, and it's all thanks to this nifty little gadget.

These devices will do all the hard work for you while you kick back and relax. And with many of them being remote-controlled, you can even start your cleaning session from the comfort of your lounge chair.

Robot pool cleaners are typically equipped with brushes, jets, and other tools to help them clean pools quickly and efficiently. Plus, they usually have sensors that alert owners when the pool needs cleaning, so you can avoid having to deal with messy pools on your hands all summer long.

With so many home robots to help with chores, it was only a matter of time before one was designed to take care of your pool too.

2. Smart Water Monitor System

Relaxing by the pool is all fun and games until someone forgets to check the chlorine levels, and everyone ends up with red, itchy eyes.

With a smart water monitor system in place, you can avoid these stressful situations and keep your chlorine levels in check at all times.

This system works by constantly monitoring the water quality and sends alerts to your smartphone or tablet when it's time to add more chemicals. That way, you can always have peace of mind knowing that your pool is in tip-top shape and ready for summer fun.

It also has a feature that allows you to set a schedule for the system to automatically adjust the pH levels and chlorine levels based on the time of day and weather conditions. Why waste time fiddling with the chlorine levels when you can let the system do it for you?

3. Pool Alarm System

This next gadget is a must-have for any pool owner, especially if you have young children or pets.

A pool alarm system will alert you whenever someone or something enters the water, so you can quickly take action if necessary.

Many of these systems come with additional features like motion sensors and alarms that sound when someone tries to access the pool area without permission. That way, you can keep your family and belongings safe while enjoying some time by the pool.

Some pool alarm systems can send alerts to your smartphone or tablet, so you can always be in the know, no matter where you are.

Don't take chances when it comes to pool safety. Invest in a quality pool alarm system and enjoy some peace of mind all summer long.

4. Waterproof Wireless Speaker

Wireless speakers are a must-have for any summer gathering, and they're especially handy when you're spending time by the pool.

With a waterproof wireless speaker, you can take your music anywhere without worrying about it getting wet or damaged.

These types of speakers are designed to withstand the elements, so you can enjoy your tunes even when you're in the water.

There are many different styles and sizes of waterproof wireless speakers on the market, so you're sure to find one that's perfect for your needs.

From floating speakers that are great for pool parties to ones that can be mounted on your deck or patio, there's a waterproof speaker out there for everyone.

5. Smart Pool Cover

A pool cover is a must-have for any pool owner. Not only does it help to keep your pool clean, but it also prevents leaves and other debris from getting into the water.

Smart pool covers offer all the same benefits as traditional pool covers, but with the added bonus of being able to be controlled with your smartphone or tablet.

With a smart pool cover, you can open and close your pool with the tap of a button. That way, you never have to worry about forgetting to close your pool when it's not in use.

Plus, many smart pool covers come with sensors that can detect when someone or something is on the cover. That way, you can rest assured that your pool is always safe and sound.

By easily being able to open and close your pool with the tap of a button, you'll be able to save time and energy all summer long—no more water loss from evaporation or energy costs from constantly reheating your pool.

It's a cost-saving, time-saving, and stress-free way to enjoy your pool all summer long.

6. Smart Pool Heater

Tired of waiting for your pool to heat up before you can take a dip? With a smart pool heater, you'll never have to wait for your pool to reach the perfect temperature again.

With a smart pool heater, you can easily control the temperature of your pool from your smartphone or tablet. That way, you can always keep your pool at the perfect temperature for swimming, no matter what the weather is like outside.

It's the perfect way to plan ahead and make sure your pool is always ready for a swim, no matter what the weather brings. It's also a simpler and more efficient way to heat your pool, so you'll save energy and money in the long run.

7. Smart Swim Goggles

Do you have a hard time keeping track of your laps when you're swimming? With a pair of smart swim goggles, you'll never have to worry about losing count again.

Smart swim goggles are equipped with sensors that track your laps and distance as you swim. They also include features like pace alerts and lap timers, so you can always stay on track.

Plus, many smart swim goggles come with built-in display screens that show your progress as you swim. That way, you can easily see how far you've come and how much further you have to go.

With smart swim goggles, you'll be able to focus on your swimming and reach your fitness goals in no time. Offering similar health metrics as the Fitbit or Apple Watch, it's no wonder that smart swim goggles are becoming increasingly popular among swimmers of all levels.

A Smarter Pool for a Better Summer

Relaxing by the pool is one of the best parts of summer. But with the right gadgets, it can be even better.

There are plenty of ways to upgrade your pool this summer from wireless speakers to smart pool covers. So dive into the world of smart pool gadgets and make your summer even better.
