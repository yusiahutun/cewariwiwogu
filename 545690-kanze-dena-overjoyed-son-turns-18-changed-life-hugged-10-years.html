Kanze Dena Overjoyed as Son Turns 18, Discloses She Co-parents with Baby Daddy

June 2024 · 4 minute read
  • Former State House spokesperson Kanze Dena could not hide her joy after her firstborn son, Amani, turned 18
  • The mother of two showered her son with love, recounting some of the challenges they had faced, praising him for being an understanding child
  • Kanze also talked about how her 18-year-old boy relates with his stepparents and stepsiblings, revealing he makes their co-parenting job easy

Kanze Dena has penned a heartwarming message to her firstborn child, on his birthday.

How many kids does Kanze Dena have?

The former State House spokesperson and Citizen TV newsreader's son, Amani, turned 18.

Kanze had one child before she got married to lawyer Nick Mararo in 2019, whom she shares another kid with. She shared photos of her eldest son, pampering him with love.

Kanze reminisced about giving birth to Amani in 2006, narrating how he changed her life.

She talked about how he relates with his stepparents and stepsiblings, revealing he has made their co-parenting easy.

"My champion! you have made me proud. We have been through Winter, Autumn, Spring and Summer and you never changed. You winged it! when you didn't have toys like your friends, couldn't take you out to good places because we didn't have, and you embraced your stepparents and siblings like the champ you are... Never giving us co-parenting trouble!...And when God blessed us you remained constant," she wrote in part.

Kanze revealed her son's comforting words kept her going and is overjoyed to see him transition into an adult. She allowed her son to join social media after becoming an adult.

"Today you officially have permission to be on social media... I want you to know that my greatest memory was the day we watched Woman Without Limits. It was the first time I shared my battle with low self-esteem and I did mention that I doubted if I was a good mother (almost 7-8 years ago). You held my hand, hugged me for like 10 years. 'Mum you are the best mother I could ever ask for.. you are kali but you are my friend, and I know you love me. I love you mummie.' Those words came right back today as I stayed up to say another prayer for your next season," she added.

"Keep on keeping on son. Don't lose your focus on Jesus. He is your greatest anchor (you will soon discover as you navigate through life). happy birthday champ Newest voter in town...mama loves you!" she said.
