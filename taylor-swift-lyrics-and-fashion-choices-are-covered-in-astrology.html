Taylor Swift lyrics and fashion choices are covered in astrology

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Fans believe Taylor Swift is invested in her astrological compatibility with Travis Kelce as much as the Swifties hope.

Taylor Swift is a Sagittarius and whether she is a believer of astrology, fans have found plenty of hints in her song lyrics that reference various zodiac signs. The music legend also often wears accessories related to zodiac signs, like her Constellation ring or a recent zodiac clutch.

Taylor Swift’s stuns in astrology clutch

For years, fans of the Grammy-winning celebrity have debated whether Taylor Swift believes in astrology. Especially as listeners find many hints in her song lyrics. Tay Tay was spotted wearing a Naeem Khan zodiac clutch in her New York premiere of Poor Things look yesterday.

Earlier in 2017, Taylor dropped another hint of being into astrology as she wore then-boyfriend Joe Alwyn’s sign Pisces constellation ring to hint at her relationship that was kept private and away from the limelight.

Song lyrics with astrology references

Fans have decoded several of Taylor’s songs that have astrology references. Her song The Archer can be an anthem for all Sagittarius including T. Swizzle herself. The song does not only reference a bow and arrow but also talks about traits that Sagittarius folks are likely to have.

A fan on Reddit explained: “Sagittarius is a sign notorious for having commitment issues because it is a very adventurous sign. Sagittarius struggles to stay in one place because they love to explore and learn new things. It is always going out searching for the truth. It is also a fire sign, so they are very outgoing and can lack emotional understanding, which can cause a lot of conflicts with people they interact with.

“In this song, Taylor talks about struggling to keep people in her life. She talks about how the friendships she makes just come and go in her life (Easy they come, easy they go, I jump from the train, I ride off alone big) and implies that there’s a part of her that is mentally prepared for people to leave her life because there always ends up being conflict (Combat, I’m ready for combat, cause all my enemies started out as friends). Therefore, lack of commitment is a big theme in her social life.”

Another song that could reference Taylor’s past relationship with movie star Joe is Peace. She sings: “I’m a fire, and I’ll keep your brittle heart warm, when your cascade ocean wave blues come.” This could be referring to Tay being a fire sign and the British actor a water sign.

Taylor and Travis’ compatibility

Fans who believe in astrology were early to speculate Tay Tay’s new boyfriend Travis Kelce could be The 1 as astrology showed they had chances of hitting it off instantly. Later, the celebrity couple revealed their connection was stronger ahead of her appearance at the Arrowhead stadium. They actually had their first date a while before, when the world thought that was their debut.

For Taylor, the second half of 2023 has also been significant, which made her relationship with the Catching Kelce star stronger. Astrology predicted it was the time to explore ‘undiscovered avenues of pleasure’ for Sagittarius folks which she did.

Travis traveled to Argentina to see Taylor perform and the music legend honored his presence by changing her Karma lyrics in November.

Needless to say, Travis and Taylor’s Libra-Sag connection is going strong and fans might just get to hear more about it in the cultural icon’s next banger song, or an entire album!
