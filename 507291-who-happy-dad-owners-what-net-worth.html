Who is the CEO of Happy Dad?

When fans spotted Steve around the brewery, it raised the question; does Steve own Happy Dad? Happy Dad is a collective venture among friends, meaning each of the founding members is an owner and has the right to make all the important decisions.

However, the group made Sam Shahidi the CEO and John Shahidi the President. This does not necessarily make the two brothers Happy Dad owners. They settled for the two probably because of their experience and business acumen.
