Double Trouble! Gigi and Bella Hadid Pose in Nothing But Racy Lingerie

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Double trouble indeed!

Gigi Hadid took to Instagram to tease a bondage-inspired photo spread in V Magazine, where she strips down to her skivvies with her model sister, Bella Hadid.

The smokin' hot editorial, which was shot by celebrity photographer Stephen Klein and styled by Patti Wilson, features the two sisters in some very skimpy lingerie. Gigi, 20, sports a pair of knee-high latex boots, while her 18-year-old sister sports stockings and fingerless gloves.

Yolanda Foster's daughters are both sprawled on a blue fur coat, wearing killer red lipstick, bangs and a ton of black eyeliner.

Gigi is no stranger to showing a ton of skin in her modeling shots. For her breakout appearance in the SI swimsuit issue, the blonde beauty wore only skimpy bikini bottoms, and she also stripped down to her birthday suit in VMan magazine and for Tom Ford Fragrance.

Now, Bella is making a name for herself in the modeling world, gracing the pages of Glamour, Vogue Australia ﻿and more.

The younger Hadid sister recently opened up about being compared to Gigi, admitting that the two have very different personalities.

"We're very similar - we even have the same voice - but my whole life she's been the one with the good grades," she recently told Vogue Australia. "She's beautiful, smart and funny, and I've always been the one who kind of rebels."

Bella, who referred to herself as the "the black sheep of the family," even confessed to changing her outward appearance to set herself apart. "I just wanted to show people that we're different. I even dyed my hair brown," she added.

One thing's for sure, the Hadid sisters are here to stay!
