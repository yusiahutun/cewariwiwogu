Though their courtship phase has commenced since tying the knot, Rick Salomon is keeping the chivalry alive.

Knowing the way to Pamela Anderson's heart, the 46-year-old took her shopping at high-end retailer Maxfield on Thursday.

And it wasn't just to window shop.

I'll take one of each! Rick Salomon, right, showered his wife Pamela Anderson, left, with gifts as they shopped at Maxfield in Los Angeles on Thursday

Pam, 46, appeared to have picked up a lavish necklace, as well as other garments that her husband was seen carrying upon their departure from the boutique.

Perhaps seducing Rick into spending, the former Baywatch babe showed off her sculpted calves in a black mini skirt and sky-high Christian Louboutin platforms.

Her pixie 'do was coiffed voluminously and she sported a pink pout.

She's still got it! The former Baywatch babe showed off her toned legs in a black mini dress and stilettos

Like day and night, Rick was wearing a white blouse, white denim, and yellow boots.

The scruffy-faced star also sported a backwards baseball cap and appeared be hydrating himself with a healthy drink from Pressed Juicery.

Meanwhile, Pamela currently has two film projects on the horizon including the comedy Jackhammer.

Pearl princess: The blonde bombshell showed off an extravagant new necklace

Luxurious lady: Showing off her designer taste, the actress sported Christian Louboutin heels

The blonde bombshell is billed simply as 'Groupie' in the upcoming film centred around the world of professional male stripping.

Michael Hanus not only directs but performs in the title role as he is accompanied by Guy Christie, Nicole Sullivan, and Silvana Azurdia.

Jackhammer is set for release in the US on July 1.

The Scary Movie 3 star is not just starring in raunchy comedies as she also has an upcoming role in the drama Vernon God Little.

The Werner Herzog directed film is about a Texas teenager whose best friend goes on a shooting spree at their high school.

The film also stars Sasha Pieterse, Russell Brand, Mike Tyson, Julia Sarah Stone, and Austin Abrams.
