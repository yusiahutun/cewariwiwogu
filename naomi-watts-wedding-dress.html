Naomi Watts marries in Oscar de la Renta

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Naomi Watts is the latest celebrity bride that we will be bookmarking for chic wedding inspiration. The actress tied the knot to fellow actor Billy Crudup over the weekend, and her look was pretty spectacular.

The couple had the perfect stylish city ceremony at a courthouse in Manhattan, and then partied with friends afterwards. Watts confirmed the news in an Instagram post, which she simply captioned "Hitched!" while she also shared plenty of snaps on her Stories.

For the occasion, she wore Oscar de la Renta's delicate 'Sweetheart Water Lilly Guipure' dress, which features a timeless silhouette and beautiful sheer lace detail. Watts kept her bridal look fuss-free and appealed to laidback brides everywhere.

She styled her dress with strappy Gianvito Rossi gold heels, an Anita Ko pear diamond choker, and carried a sweet bouquet from a local deli. The actress later wore cream sunglasses and a white blazer over her wedding dress.

Crudup meanwhile chose a classic navy suit with a crisp white shirt, which he wore open and with no tie.

When it came to her wedding beauty, Watts wore her mid-length blonde hair in loose relaxed waves and kept her make-up rosy, dewy and fresh. She tied everything together with the ultimate summery pink glossy lip, which worked with her delicate lace dress perfectly.

The actress' post has received plenty of well wishes and congratulations from other famous names, including the likes of Reese Witherspoon, Chloë Sevigny, Jennifer Coolidge, Gwyneth Paltrow and more, while friend Mark Ruffalo sharing a sweet snap of the happy couple.

If you feel inspired by Watts' laid-back approach to bridal dressing, then her Oscar de la Rena gown is still available to purchase or, alternatively, you can discover more bridal shops to visit that are perfect for your big day this way, as approved by our Bazaar editors.
