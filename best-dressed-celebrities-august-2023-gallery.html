57 best dressed celebrities this month: Rita Ora, Georgia May Jagger &amp; more

February 2024 · 11 minute read

Saddle up for a month of racing style, festival get ups and summer suits from the stylish stars like Maya Jama, Beyoncé and more

    With the glitz and glamour of awards season firmly in hibernation until next winter, the arrival of August brings a saturation of summer street style, casual get ups and holiday ensembles to our social feeds from the celebrity style set.

    From raucous ruffled mini dresses to slick tailored suits, the return of corsets and the renaissance of Y2K dressing dominating trends, this summer has already served up some seriously enviable looks from our favourite celebrities, from Beyonce to Maya Jama. With Notting Hill Carnival, the Glorious Goodwood, music festivals galore and Copenhagen Fashion Week on the agenda, August will no doubt welcome a blanket of best-dressed stars through its doors.

    Rita Ora

    Rita Ora strutted her stuff in the streets of Venice rocking low-slung leather trousers and a sheer lace crop top. She accessorised with Roberto Bravo jewellery and slung a leather trench over her shoulders.

    Georgia May Jagger

    Georgia May Jagger ensured she took centre stage at thefilm premiere during the 80th Venice International Film Festival. Looking beautiful in a sheer gown with shimmering sequin detailing, the model daughter of Mick Jagger commanded attention as she glided down the red carpet.

    Hailey Bieber 

    Strawberry girl incoming! Rhode beauty founder Hailey Bieber was a lady in red to launch her newest peptide lip treatment, a sheer berry red gloss inspired by Krispy Kreme's strawberry glaze donut. 

    The 26-year-old It-girl is taking her self-proclaimed "strawberry girl summer" extremely seriously, and we cannot get enough of her dreamy kitsch fits and berry red accessories this month. 

    Hailey also enjoyed a low-key night out in Los Angeles, rocking a flattering leather mini dress. Justin Bieber's wife looked gorgeous in her eye-catching design, teamed with pointed-toe stilettos.

    Michelle Obama

    The former First Lady of the United States, Michelle Obama, stepped onto the court at the US Open in a dreamy denim Oscar de la Renta dress and heeled wedges. Chanelling fifties glamour, the bestselling author amped up her look with a sky-high pony tail and dewy makeup.

    Demi Moore

    Demi Moore was the queen of casual as she stepped out in Beverley Hills in an all-white ensemble, letting her Rapunzel-like tresses fall past her waist. 

    Venus Williams

    Tennis legend Venus Williams debuted pastel pink hair at the Citi Taste of Tennis New York City 2023 event, rocking a black 'Pink Lady' jacket and casual pants. 

    Dylan Mulvaney

    'Days of Girlhood' star Dylan Mulvaney was saluted on Sunday night as she took home Breakout Creator at the 2023 Streamy Awards. A timeless beauty in a fitted satin mini dress, Dylan twirled onto the stage in a slick red gown adorned with an oversized bow across the bust. She slipped into glittering cherry-red heels and levelled up her evening wear with a set of sparkling diamond accessories. 

    Earlier this month, TikTok royalty Dylan was a total goddess in an oversized blue blazer dress and towering strappy heels.

    Amal Clooney

    Giving the term ‘airport chic’ a new definition, Amal Clooney looked breathtaking as she stepped off the plane in Venice rocking a thigh-skimming mini dress and towering slingback heels. 

    Beyoncé

    Beyoncé looked sensational as she continued to dominate the stage during her Renaissance tour. Rocking fishnet tights and a leather bodysuit, the 'Heated' singer stole the show with her incredible stage presence. 

    The singer's unrivalled tour wardrobe reigned supreme as the music legend hit the stage earlier this month. Dressed to the nines in a monochrome gown, silk opera gloves and towering platform heels, Beyoncé was a blonde bombshell with waist-length, platinum extensions.

    Rebel Wilson

    Epitomising timeless glamour, Rebel Wilson was preppy and pristine in a Gucci mini dress to attend the premiere of at Sydney Opera House. 

    Jennifer Lawrence

    Serving up modelesque energy, Oscar winner Jennifer Lawrence strutted through New York City in wide-leg balloon pants and towering heels. 

    Leigh-Anne Pinnock

    Little Mix songstress Leigh-Anne Pinnock made another appearance this month for her first live showcase as a solo artist at The Pergola on the Wharf in London. Stealing the spotlight in a lime yellow wrap skirt and fitted bodice dress, the former star served up popstar energy as she proved her sartorial prowess. 

    Venus Williams

    Venus Williams made a case for athleisure chic when she attended the Citi Taste of Tennis New York City 2023 event at Gotham Hall in NYC. The tennis superstar rocked low-slung sweatpants, a baseball cap and a slogan blazer to attend the event in style.

    South Korean actress Lim Ji-Yeon aka Im Ji-Yeon was a total lady in red at the 'LOEWE' 2023 F/W collection on 22 August in Seoul, South Korea. The star's two-tone, feminine tea dress was paired with a charming black clutch bag and sophisticated cream mules.

    Heidi Klum

    German-American supermodel Heidi Klum stepped out in New York City suited and booted in a silky patterned pantsuit. Chanelling , the America's Got Talent judge was a retro dream in her 70s print co-ord. 

    Halle Bailey

    Halle Bailey was the picture of cool at the Chlöe x Halle and VS PINK design collaboration launch, hosted at Casita Hollywood in LA. The star rocked an oversized leather blazer and sporty bralette. 

    Hilary Duff

    Hilary Duff proved active wear can be effortless as she was pictured stomping about Hollywood in sporty leggings, an oversized sweater and black sneakers - all whilst armed with a large Chanel crossbody bag. Iconinc Lizzie McGuire energy. 

    Angelina Jolie

    Angelina Jolie was the picture of elegance in a flowing black dress and monochrome bag as she enjoyed a low-key outing in the East Village. Nailing off-duty NYC style, the A-list actress and activist completed her look with oversized sunglasses.

    Lily Allen

    Lily Allen looked so chic in a black one-shoulder gown adorned with red sequin embroidery as she left the Duke of York's Theatre after a showing of. The West End star's dress wrapped around her neck for a choker-style finish, and Lily accessorised with a pair of gold statement drop earrings to complete the glam look.

    Jessica Alba

    Jessica Alba was a double denim delight in a flared jumpsuit and tailored blazer with 80s inspired epaulettes. The actress toted a white leather bag over one shoulder and added a touch of sparkle with hoop earrings for her appearance.

    Jennifer Lopez

    Jennifer Lopez nailed off-duty chic in leggings and a fitted tee while leaving a hotel in Beverly Hills. Looking super cool in her casual sportswear, JLo accessorized with oversized shades.

    Winnie Harlow

    Model Winnie Harlow made a case for the crochet trend in a thigh-split, rainbow hued skirt and matching hat to attend the Lori Harvey's Yevrah Swim launch at Villa Fiona in Hollywood. The former star added a canary yellow Chanel bag and dressed her look down with box-fresh sneakers.

    Olivia Rodrigo

    Pop princess Olivia Rodrigo touched down in London this week, and her wardrobe is 100 per cent It-girl approved. The 20-year-old was snapped leaving BBC Radio Studios on 15 August in a mohair mini dress and knee-high boots. 

    Kelly Rowland

    Kelly Rowland looked sublime in a two-tone dress to attend the Black Excellence Brunch at Serra on Vine in Los Angeles. The wrapped bodice of her silhouette-skimming dress consisted of a pastel pink satinbow, which cascaded into a sheer embellished skirt. 

    Padma Lakshmi

    It was impossible to miss Emmy award-winning TV host Padma Lakshmi as she strutted through the streets of New York in a hot pink velvet three piece suit ahead of her appearance on 'Good Morning America'. Stunning!

    Martha Hunt

    The Cinq à Sept Hamptons dinner dress code was no match for Victoria's Secret angel Martha Hunt, who exuded angelic elegance in a dreamy pink ruffled dress. 

    Ciara 

    It's been a whirlwind month for Ciara. The "Level Up" singer not only shared that she was expecting her fourth child, her third with husband Russell Wilson, but she also debuted a new music video for her song "How We Roll". Embracing her baby bump at the in New York, the star donned a stunning oversized black shirt dress and strappy heels to complete her glowy maternity ensemble.

    star Sydney Sweeney went hell for leather as she graced the red carpet at the Variety Power of Young Hollywood event in Los Angeles. Opting for a peplum latex bodice and a vampy leather maxi skirt, the actress made a case for monochrome dressing.

    Hailee Steinfeld

    Hailee Steinfeld was every inch an It-girl as she rocked a strapless waistcoat and and matching cream pants to join the Core Hydration - Where Balance Begins panel in New York City.

    Gabrielle Union

    Gabrielle Union-Wade turned up the heat in a timeless LBD for her husband Dwyane Wade's Hall of Fame initiation party at Chateau Marmont. The actress accessorised with the Sam Icon KSNYL Nylon Mini Pochette from Kate Spade. Satin, silhouette-skimming, sartorial perfection.  

    Amelia Dimoldenberg

    star Amelia Dimoldenberg served up a seriously slick look for the GRM Gala 2023 at Sky Garden, turning heads with her plunging blazer dress adorned with witchy fringing and a slick, high top knot. 

    Grece Ghanem

    Even if you don't recognise her name, you'll likely have seen Grece Ghanem's ageless style taking the Internet by storm. The former microbiologist-turned-blogger epitomised effortless dressing at Copenhagen Fashion Week wearing a military-style mini skirt, vampy black boots, a vintage silk cami and a Louis Vuitton bag. 

    Nelly Furtado 

    Nelly Furtado was seriously stylish in a pair of loose-fit cut-out jeans and a white crop top as she stepped out of Sony Studios in New York. She rounded off the Y2K look with a pair of oversized ski-style sunglasses and chunky black trainers - and the singer looked so effortlessly cool.

    Elizabeth Hurley slipped into a plunging scarlet gown with a breathtaking train to attend the Remus Lifestyle Night hosted at the Sea Hotel Aya in Palma de Mallorca. Complete with ruffled sleeves and side-split skirt, the look was perfection.

    Supermodel Emily Ratajkowski paired her summer slip dress with the ultimate leather boot, making a case for knee-high heels as she strutted through New York City on Wednesday 2 August.

    Maya Jama couldn't have chosen a better ensemble to wrap the tenth season of Love Island. Epitomising It-girl glamour, the 28-year-old Vogue cover girl donned a vampy ruffled corset dress from AADNEVIK to host the final.

    Nicola Peltz Beckham 

    Mrs Peltz Beckham has perfected the art of effortless dressing, so it comes as no surprise the billionaire heiress oozed sartorial luxury for a boat trip with her husband Brooklyn and her parents this weekend.
