Alix Earle goes red carpet official with 'mystery man' after months of speculation

June 2024 · 3 minute read

After months of ‘soft launching’ him over on TikTok, Alix Earle has finally made her red carpet debut with her ‘mystery’ man at the ESPYs. Although the announcement may not come as a surprise to many fans, who worked out his identity from her many social media videos.

The 22-year-old University of Miami grad recently became one of the fastest-growing creators on the platform TikTok and has now amassed over 5 million followers. She’s known for her iconic GRWM videos, and of course, she had to make one whilst getting ready to go ‘official’ at the biggest sporting red carpet event of the year.

Alix Earle teases Braxton Berrios on TikTok

The pair were first spotted together in May when a fan posted a video meeting the influencer, with Braxton stood by her side. Another TikTok user then spotted him in a video from Alix Earle‘s graduation celebration.

They’re then spotted at a basketball game together at the start of June, however, the TikToker replied said she “doesn’t have a boyfriend.” However, she did confirm the two were together at a game.

As the weeks went on, Braxton continued to be “soft launched” on Alix’s TikTok as they took a trip to the Hamptons together.

First, it was a mystery man’s arm, and then his whole body was shown as the two shared an embrace, however, his face was still covered.

Although by then, it had become pretty obvious to fans who the ‘mystery man’ was.

Alix Earle and Braxton Berrios go red carpet official

Alix Earle and Miami Dolphins player Braxton Berrios made their red-carpet debut at this year’s ESPY Awards. Earle stunned in a floor-length leather dress, while her partner looked swag in a pastel pink suit paired with a pair of fresh white sneakers.

Although many fans had clicked on who the ‘mystery man’ was in Alix’s videos, for the ones who were still unsure, the case is now solved.

Alix herself was also aware her fan base had clocked onto her relationship, as pointed out in her signature GRWM for the red carpet.

At the start of the video, titled GRWM ESPYS the 22-year-old said: “I’m going as a plus one…. You can probably guess who I’m going with.”

The TikToker stunned at the Barbie premiere

It looks like Alix has had a busy week of red carpets, as she recently stunned at the first world premiere of the Barbie movie earlier this week.

Many celebrities attended the premiere, including Nicki Minaj and Dua Lipa, who stunned in a sizzling sheer dress.

The star stunned in a glamorous gold dress where fans said she looked like a “real-life Barbie.”
